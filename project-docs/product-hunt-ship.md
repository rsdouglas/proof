### Description (shown on Ship page — 150-300 words)

> Most small businesses know they need testimonials. The hard part is collecting them.
> 
> Vouch is a simple tool that makes it effortless to collect authentic testimonials from your customers and display them on your website — without chasing anyone down or copying and pasting from emails.
> 
> **How it works:**
> 1. Share your collection link with happy customers
> 2. They submit a testimonial in under 2 minutes
> 3. You approve it from your dashboard
> 4. It shows up on your site automatically
> 
> **One script tag.** No third-party platform. No monthly subscription required to get started.
> 
> **Why Vouch over Trustpilot, Senja, or Google Reviews?**
> - You own your testimonials — they're not locked in a third-party platform
> - $9/month vs $29–$99/month for the competition
> - Displays anywhere: any website, any builder, any stack
> - Google rich results schema built in — your star ratings show in search
> 
> **Free forever for 1 widget.** No credit card required.
> 
> We're in beta and actively building. Your feedback shapes the product.
