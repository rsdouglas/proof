# Collecting Testimonials

SocialProof makes it easy to collect real, verified testimonials from your customers — two ways.

## Method 1: Email a customer directly (recommended)

The fastest way to get your first testimonial is to email a happy customer directly from the SocialProof dashboard.

**In the dashboard:**
1. Go to **Testimonials** → click **Request testimonial**
2. Enter the customer's email (required) and name (optional)
3. Choose which widget to link them to (if you have multiple)
4. Add a personal note (optional but strongly recommended — it dramatically improves response rates)
5. Click **Send request**

SocialProof sends a branded email with a direct link to your collection form. The customer clicks, fills it out in under 2 minutes, and submits.

**Why this beats other approaches:**
- One click from the dashboard — no copy-pasting links
- The email arrives from your business name, not a generic platform
- Personal notes ("Hi Sarah, it was so great working with you last month...") get 3x more responses than blank requests

**What to write in the personal note:**
> "Hi [Name], it was such a pleasure working with you on [project]. Your testimonial would really help other [clients/customers] know what to expect. Takes 2 minutes — I really appreciate it."

## Method 2: Share your collector link

Every SocialProof account gets a unique hosted collector page — something like:
`https://collect.socialproof.dev/your-business-name`

This page is:
- Mobile-friendly
- Branded with your business name
- Fully hosted — no setup required

**Best places to share it:**

**After a purchase:** "Thanks for your order! If you have a moment, I'd love to hear what you think: [link]"

**After a service delivery:** "It was great working with you. A quick testimonial would mean a lot: [link]"

**In your email signature:** Add "Happy with my work? [Share a testimonial]" as a small footer link.

**In a follow-up email (3–7 days later):** People remember you better a few days out. A simple "How did it go?" works well.

**In a post-purchase SMS or DM:** For e-commerce or social sellers — direct message after delivery with the link.

## What customers see

The form asks for:
1. Their name
2. A rating (1–5 stars, optional)
3. Their testimonial (free text)
4. Title / company (optional)

Short. Respectful of their time. Usually takes under 2 minutes.

## Approving testimonials

When a submission comes in, you'll see it in your dashboard under **Testimonials → Pending**.

Review it. If it looks good, click **Approve** and it will appear in your widget automatically.
You can also:
- Edit it (with customer permission)
- Add a star rating if they didn't leave one
- Tag it by product/service
- Decline it (it won't be shown anywhere)

## Exporting testimonials

Go to **Testimonials** → **Export CSV** to download all your testimonials as a spreadsheet. Useful for:
- Sharing with your team
- Using testimonial text in ads or emails
- Keeping a backup

## Tips

- **Ask within 48 hours** of a positive interaction — enthusiasm is still fresh.
- **Make it personal.** "A testimonial from you would mean a lot" beats "Please review us."
- **Start with your 5 best customers.** Getting 5 strong testimonials fast looks better than 20 mediocre ones.
- **Send the email from SocialProof** rather than forwarding a link — it's a cleaner experience for the customer and tracks clicks for you.
