1. Go to **community.shopify.com**
2. Search for threads using the queries below
3. Read the thread fully — pick the template that fits the question
4. **Customize the first 1-2 sentences** to reference something specific in that post
5. Post as a community member, not a brand rep
6. Check back in 24h — reply to any follow-up questions

**Rules of engagement:**
- Read Shopify Community guidelines before first post
- Karma-build first: upvote helpful posts, answer easy questions before dropping the tool mention

## TEMPLATE 1 — "How do I collect / display testimonials on my Shopify store?"

> **Best for:** Direct "how do I show testimonials" questions. Most common ask.

---

[CUSTOMIZE: Reference something specific from the thread, e.g. "Sounds like you want testimonials you actually control, not just pulled from a third-party platform — totally different thing."]

There are two approaches depending on your budget:

**Free / manual:**
Text your 3 happiest customers: *"Would you send me 2–3 sentences I could put on my site?"* Most will. Paste the responses directly into your theme's custom HTML section (Shopify's built-in editor handles this fine).

**More scalable:**
If you want a proper collection form + approval workflow + embed snippet, [SocialProof](https://socialproof.dev) does exactly that for free. You share a link → customer fills in their name and a paragraph → you approve it → one `<script>` tag puts it on your store. Free plan covers one widget, no credit card.

The key thing is placement: put testimonials **above the fold on your homepage** or **right next to your Add to Cart button** — not on a separate Reviews page that nobody clicks to.

Happy to share where in the Shopify theme editor to drop the embed if that's helpful.

For testimonials specifically (as opposed to product star ratings), there's a lighter-weight option worth knowing about.

[SocialProof](https://socialproof.dev) focuses on text testimonials — the kind you put in your hero section or on an About page to build trust with new visitors. ## TEMPLATE 3 — "How do I build trust with new visitors / increase conversion rate?"

> **Best for:** CRO / trust questions. Often asked by stores 0-6 months in with no social proof yet.

---

[CUSTOMIZE: Reference where they say they're losing people, e.g. "If bounce rate is high on your homepage, the trust gap is usually the issue — visitors don't see anyone like them vouching for you."]

The highest-leverage trust signal for a new store: real customer words on your site, placed where people make decisions.

The playbook that works:
1. Message your first 10 customers directly — "Would you send me 2 sentences I could put on my website?"
2. Put those quotes **above your hero CTA** and **next to your pricing or Add to Cart**
3. Refresh every 3 months with new ones

This outperforms generic trust badges for most small stores because visitors are looking for *people like them* who bought and were happy.

If you want to set up a proper collection flow so customers can submit without you chasing them, [SocialProof](https://socialproof.dev) has a free plan — you share a link, they fill in a form, you approve it, one embed script. Takes about 20 minutes to set up end to end.

The manual version is also fine if you're just starting out. Either way — get those quotes on your homepage this week.

## TEMPLATE 4 — "Embed code question / how to add custom HTML to Shopify"

> **Best for:** Technical threads about adding scripts or custom code to Shopify themes.

---

[CUSTOMIZE: Reference the specific technical question, e.g. "For adding a script to your header in Dawn theme, the process changed slightly in 2.0..."]

For a testimonials widget specifically, the embed goes in one of two places depending on where you want it:

**Homepage section (recommended):** In your theme editor → Add section → Custom HTML → paste the embed `<script>` tag. This works in all Shopify 2.0 themes including Dawn.

**Inline on a product page:** Theme editor → Products template → Add block → Custom liquid → same embed tag.

If you're using [SocialProof](https://socialproof.dev), they have a full Shopify-specific embed guide here: [socialproof.dev/docs/embed-guide](https://socialproof.dev/docs/embed-guide) — covers both Dawn and older themes, plus how to style it to match your store.

The embed is a single `<script>` tag so there's nothing to maintain after setup — it pulls the widget from their CDN.

## TEMPLATE 5 — "Word of mouth is my main channel / I rely on referrals"

> **Best for:** Merchants describing their business model as referral-heavy, looking to scale it.

---

[CUSTOMIZE: Acknowledge the referral strength, e.g. "Word of mouth is the best channel — the question is how to capture it so it keeps working even when you're not in the room."]

The gap in most word-of-mouth businesses: the testimonials exist in DMs, emails, and texts — but they never make it onto the website where new visitors can see them.

Easiest fix: create a single form link and send it to your happiest customers. Ask for 2–3 sentences. Put whatever you get above your contact form, next to your pricing, in your email footer.

I've been using [SocialProof](https://socialproof.dev) for this — it handles the collection (shareable form link), lets you approve before publishing, and gives you a one-line embed. Free for one widget. The whole setup takes maybe 20 minutes.

The business case: a first-time visitor who reads a specific quote from someone like them will convert at 2–3× the rate of one who sees generic marketing copy. Your referral channel is already creating those quotes — capturing them just makes the business compound.
