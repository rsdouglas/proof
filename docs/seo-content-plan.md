# SEO Content Plan

## Goal

Own long-tail search traffic from small business owners searching for trust/review solutions. These are high-intent searches from people who are ready to solve a problem.

## Target audience

- Shopify store owners who feel "unprofessional"
- Freelancers who struggle to get testimonials from clients
- Coaches / consultants who want social proof but feel awkward asking
- Local service businesses who have good reviews on Google but nothing on their site

## Content pillars

### 1. Testimonial collection
*Intent: how do I get testimonials from customers?*

Posts:
- "How to Ask Clients for Testimonials Without Being Awkward (7 Scripts That Work)"
- "The Best Time to Ask for a Testimonial (And How to Make It Easy)"
- "Testimonial Request Email Templates for Freelancers"
- "Why Most Business Owners Don't Ask for Testimonials — And How to Fix That"

### 2. Social proof and conversion
*Intent: how does social proof help my business?*

Posts:
- "Social Proof for Small Businesses: What Works, What Doesn't"
- "How to Use Testimonials to Double Your Conversion Rate"
- "The Psychology Behind Why Testimonials Work (And How to Use It)"
- "What Customers Actually Want to See Before They Buy From You"

### 3. Technical setup (high-intent)
*Intent: how do I add testimonials to my website?*

Posts:
- "How to Add a Testimonial Widget to Your Shopify Store (No Code)"
- "How to Embed Customer Reviews on Your Squarespace Site"
- "The Easiest Way to Display Testimonials on Any Website"
- "Trustpilot vs Vouch: Which Is Right for Your Small Business?"

### 4. Examples and inspiration
*Intent: what should my testimonials look like?*

Posts:
- "15 Testimonial Page Examples That Actually Convert (And What Makes Them Work)"
- "What Makes a Testimonial Compelling? The Anatomy of a Great Review"
- "How Coaches and Consultants Use Social Proof to Win More Clients"

## Publishing cadence

- 2 posts/month to start
- Focus on SEO quality over quantity — 1000+ words, genuinely useful
- Internal links: each post links back to the homepage or signup

## Distribution

- Share each post in relevant subreddits (r/smallbusiness, r/freelance, r/entrepreneur) — not promotional, just useful
- Pin in relevant Shopify forum threads where people ask about reviews
- Repurpose as LinkedIn posts (solopreneur audience is active there)

## Metrics

- Organic traffic to blog posts
- Click-through rate from posts to signup
- Keyword rankings for target terms (track with Google Search Console)
