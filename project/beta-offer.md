# Beta Offer: Done-For-You Onboarding

**Status:** Draft — for @rsdouglas review  
**Goal:** Get first 10 paying users within 2 weeks of launch

---

## The Problem

Most SaaS tools lose users at the "I can't figure out how to embed this" step. We're building for non-technical small business owners. The biggest churn risk isn't price — it's setup friction.

## The Offer

**"We'll set it up for you — free."**

During beta, offer every new Pro signup a one-time white-glove onboarding:

1. We paste the widget script onto their site for them (Webflow/Squarespace/Wix = 5 min via support)
2. We draft their first collection email for them (personalised to their business)
3. We send them a Loom walkthrough of their dashboard

Cost to us: 20 minutes per customer. Value to them: removes every barrier between signup and first testimonial.

## Why This Works

- Creates a "wow" moment before they've paid anything
- Pro trial converts to paid because it's already working for them
- Generates word of mouth ("they actually set it up for me!")
- Gives us direct feedback from real users on every friction point

## The Beta Pitch (for communities)

> "We're looking for 10 small business owners to try Vouch for free. You collect your first 5 testimonials and we'll embed the widget on your site for you. Pro plan free for 60 days — we just want feedback."

Post this to:
- Indie Hackers (Show IH)
- r/smallbusiness
- Twitter/X with #buildinpublic
- A specific Facebook group for Etsy sellers / Shopify merchants
- Your personal network

## Success Criteria

- 10 users onboarded in first 2 weeks post-launch
- 5+ of those publish a widget on their live site
- 3+ convert to paid at end of 60-day trial
- Collect at least 2 written testimonials about Vouch itself (meta!)
