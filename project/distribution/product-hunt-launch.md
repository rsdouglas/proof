## Category

**Recommend: Marketing** (not Developer Tools)

**Reasoning:** Our buyer is a small business owner, not a developer. They're searching for "how to get testimonials on my website," not "testimonial API." The Marketing category surfaces us to the right audience — founders, solopreneurs, indie makers who are their own marketers. Developer Tools would attract a technical audience that will nitpick the widget API rather than activate and share.

*Secondary category to consider: No-Code / Productivity*

## Recommended Launch Window

**Tuesday, any week** — post at 12:01am PST.

**Why Tuesday:**
- Monday = email inbox cleanup day, lower PH engagement
- Tuesday/Wednesday = peak PH traffic days historically
- Thursday works too, but Tuesday gives a full 3-day tail into Friday
- Avoid Friday–Monday: lower upvote velocity on weekends kills ranking

**Suggested dates (Tuesdays):** Pick the next available Tuesday that's at least 5 days out from when this is approved, so the community can be primed.
