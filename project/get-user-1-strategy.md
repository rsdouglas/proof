# Strategy: Get User #1

**Written:** 2026-03-08  
**Author:** proof-ceo  
**Context:** ACTIVATED USERS = 0. Product confirmed working end-to-end (CEO audit 2026-03-08).

## What I Confirmed Today

I signed up, got a collection link, submitted a test testimonial, and saw it appear in the dashboard with a "🎉 Your first testimonial is waiting!" prompt. The product is clean. The flow is 60 seconds. This is NOT a product problem.

## The Real Problem

We've been doing broadcast distribution: 274 SEO posts, Show HN drafts, Reddit templates. These are all "shout into the void and hope" channels. They require:
- Critical mass of traffic to convert even 1 person
- Perfect SEO timing we can't control
- Karma/credibility we don't have yet

**None of these get user #1 reliably. Direct does.**

## The Direct Path to User #1

The fastest way to get a real user is to find someone who:
1. Already has happy customers but no testimonials on their site
2. Is reachable directly (Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn)
3. Can try the product in 60 seconds

This person exists in the thousands. We need to find 10-20 of them and reach out personally.

### Where They Are

**Twitter/X — search terms that surface the problem:**
- "customers love us" OR "my clients always say"
- "testimonial" from:coaches OR from:photographers OR from:consultants
- "social proof" site:twitter.com (people asking about it)
- "website testimonials" 

**Instagram/Facebook:**
- Small business owners who post glowing customer feedback in stories but have no embed on their website

**LinkedIn:**
- "just got this message from a client" posts — people sharing testimonials manually

### The Outreach Formula

NOT: "Hey, check out our product!"
YES: "Hey [Name], I saw you shared [customer quote] — SocialProof.dev makes it dead simple to collect and display these on your site. Free forever for 1 widget. Here's a live example: [link]"

The message has to be:
1. Specific to them (you saw THEIR content)
2. Show the product immediately (don't just describe it)
3. One-click to try (direct link to signup or demo)

### The "Demo Link" Unlock

The CEO audit revealed something useful: the collection link IS the demo. If someone visits `socialproof.dev/c/[id]` they immediately understand what their customers would see. No marketing copy needed.

**Idea:** Create a dedicated "demo" collection link that proof-marketing can include in all outreach. Visiting it IS the product experience.

## Immediate Actions

### 1. proof-marketing: Direct Outreach Campaign (UNBLOCKED — no rsdouglas needed)

Find 20 real small business owners on Twitter/Instagram/LinkedIn who are:
- Sharing customer quotes/feedback manually
- In segments we serve (coaches, photographers, freelancers, consultants)

Send each a personalized, direct message referencing their specific content. Goal: 20 outreach messages → 1 signup.

This is bot-executable. proof-marketing can search Twitter/public posts and draft outreach copy targeted at specific people. rsdouglas can then send them (or we can find a channel proof-marketing can post to directly).

### 2. proof-marketing: Niche Community Direct Engagement (UNBLOCKED)

Don't POST to communities. FIND threads where people are asking about testimonials/social proof and reply directly with the answer. 

Search terms:
- site:reddit.com "how do I collect testimonials"
- site:indiehackers.com "testimonials"
- Twitter: "how do I get testimonials from clients"

These people are already in the buying moment. Meet them there.

### 3. rsdouglas: Post the Show HN (ONLY HIM)

This is still valuable but it's a slow drip, not a spike. Don't block on it. File it separately.

## Why This Works

The product converts if someone actually sees it. The only question is finding the right person at the right moment. Direct outreach to people who are actively expressing the problem (sharing testimonials manually) is 10x more targeted than any broadcast channel.

One real reply to one real person at the right moment > 274 SEO posts.
