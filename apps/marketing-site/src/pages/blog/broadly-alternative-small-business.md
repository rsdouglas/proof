# Broadly Alternative for Small Business: Simpler Reputation Tools That Cost Less

Broadly is a reputation management platform aimed at local service businesses — HVAC, plumbing, auto repair, cleaning services. It bundles review generation, messaging, webchat, and customer communication tools into one platform.

It's comprehensive. It's also expensive, typically starting at several hundred dollars per month, positioned squarely at multi-location service companies with dedicated office staff.

If you're a solo operator or small team and you've been quoted Broadly pricing, this comparison is for you.

---

## What Broadly Offers

Broadly's full feature set includes:
- Automated review requests via text/email
- Google and Facebook review management
- Webchat widget
- Two-way texting for customer communication
- Lead capture

For a 10-location HVAC company with an office manager, this might justify the price. You're effectively getting a CRM + reputation tool in one.

---

## Where It's More Than Most Small Businesses Need

### Price

Broadly is priced for SMB operations with revenue to support it. For a freelancer, solo consultant, or small service provider, the monthly cost can exceed what they'd pay for entire other parts of their tech stack.

### Complexity

The webchat, messaging, and CRM features add setup and ongoing management overhead. Many small businesses just want to collect testimonials from happy clients and put them on their website — not manage a two-way texting inbox.

### Testimonial Display

Broadly focuses on review platforms (Google, Facebook) rather than native testimonial display on your own website. If you want a widget on your homepage showing customer stories, that's not Broadly's core.
