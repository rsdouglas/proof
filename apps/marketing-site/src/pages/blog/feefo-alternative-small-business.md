# Feefo Alternative: Why Small Businesses Need More Than a Review Platform

Feefo is a verified reviews platform trusted by thousands of brands in the UK and beyond. The "verified" angle is the core value proposition — reviews are tied to actual purchases, which lends credibility.

For enterprise e-commerce brands running high transaction volumes, Feefo's verified reviews carry weight. For small business owners, it's more than necessary, and the pricing reflects the enterprise positioning.

---

## What Feefo Does Well

- **Verified purchase reviews** — only real customers who transacted can leave a review
- **Review display widgets** — aggregated star ratings, customer comments
- **SEO integration** — review schema markup for search snippets
- **Industry certifications** — Feefo Gold, Trusted Service Awards for qualifying businesses

---

## The Small Business Mismatch

### Pricing

Feefo is priced for e-commerce brands doing meaningful transaction volume. Small businesses often find the entry-level tiers more expensive than the value they get.

### Review vs. Testimonial

Feefo collects star rating reviews. SocialProof collects full text testimonials — stories, quotes, detailed outcomes. Reviews say "4 stars." Testimonials say "Sarah transformed my business in 30 days." For service businesses, the story is the conversion tool.

### Purchase-Only Limitation

You can only request Feefo reviews from verified purchasers through their system. If you want to collect a testimonial from a client you worked with last year, a speaking engagement attendee, or someone who emailed you a compliment — that's outside Feefo's workflow.
