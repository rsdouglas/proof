# Loox Alternative for Small Business: Beyond Shopify-Only Reviews

Loox is one of the most popular review apps in the Shopify App Store. Photo reviews, automated collection emails, customizable widgets — it's well-built and popular for good reason.

But Loox has a fundamental limitation: it's Shopify-specific. And even for Shopify stores, it's optimized for product reviews, not service testimonials.

---

## What Loox Does Well

Loox is built for:
- **Shopify product reviews** — photo and video reviews on product pages
- **Automated post-purchase email sequences** — email customers after delivery requesting a photo review
- **Review widgets on product pages** — star ratings, review carousels
- **Referral programs** — reward customers for leaving reviews

If you run a Shopify store selling physical products and want photo-rich product reviews, Loox is excellent.

---

## Where Loox Falls Short

### Shopify-Only

Loox only works on Shopify. If you have a Squarespace site, a WordPress blog, a custom website, or a service business — Loox doesn't help.

### Product Reviews, Not Service Testimonials

Loox is designed for "I bought this product and here's my photo review." It doesn't capture the story of "I hired this person/business and here's my experience." Service businesses — coaches, consultants, agencies, contractors — need testimonials, not star ratings.

### Pricing Starts at $9.99/Month

Loox's base plan at $9.99/month allows 250 review requests per month. The features most merchants want (unlimited requests, Google Shopping feed) are on higher tiers.

## When to Choose Each

**Choose Loox if:**
- You're on Shopify
- You sell physical products
- You want photo reviews on product pages
- Automated post-purchase review requests are your primary need

**Choose SocialProof if:**
- You're on any platform (or multiple platforms)
- You're a service business wanting customer testimonials
- You want a free option with no platform lock-in
- You need testimonials displayed on a landing page, homepage, or service page
