# Taggbox Alternative for Small Business: Testimonials vs. Social Wall Aggregation

Taggbox is a user-generated content (UGC) platform that aggregates posts from Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, YouTube, and other social channels, then displays them as a social wall on your website or at events. It's built for brands with active social followings and hashtag-driven campaigns.

For service businesses, the premise requires something most don't have: an active social community talking about them in public.

---

## The UGC Trap for Service Businesses

User-generated content tools assume users are generating content. If you're a consultant, coach, or agency, your clients' feedback rarely shows up as Instagram posts or tweets. It comes in private messages, emails, or in-person conversations.

SocialProof doesn't require your clients to post publicly. You send them a testimonial link, they respond in a structured form, and their words appear on your website. The workflow is direct, controlled, and doesn't require social media presence from either party.
