# Fera.ai Alternative for Service Businesses: Skip the E-Commerce Tools

Fera.ai is a well-regarded review and social proof platform designed specifically for e-commerce stores — primarily Shopify. It does product reviews, star ratings, photo reviews, and customer loyalty features.

If you sell physical or digital products through Shopify, Fera might be exactly right. But if you're a service provider — coach, consultant, agency, therapist, freelancer — Fera is designed for a fundamentally different business model.

---

## Products vs. Services

Product reviews answer: "Was this item as described? Did it arrive quickly? Would you buy again?"

Testimonials answer: "What was your life like before? What changed? What specific outcome did this person create for you?"

For service businesses, the testimonial is the whole pitch. You can't show a product image or a star rating. You need the story, the transformation, the specific result. That's what SocialProof is built to collect.
