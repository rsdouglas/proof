# Trustindex Alternative: Collect Your Own Testimonials, Don't Just Aggregate

Trustindex is a widget tool that aggregates your existing reviews from Google, Facebook, Yelp, Tripadvisor, and dozens of other platforms, then displays them as embeddable widgets on your website.

If you have 50+ Google reviews and want to showcase them on your homepage, Trustindex is a sensible solution. Here's when it's not the complete answer — and how SocialProof differs.

---

## What Trustindex Does Well

- **Aggregate existing reviews** — pulls in reviews you've already collected on external platforms
- **Multi-platform display** — Google + Facebook + Yelp in one unified widget
- **Automatic sync** — widget updates as new reviews come in
- **Schema markup** — helps review ratings show in Google search snippets
- **Plug-and-play** — works with WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Squarespace

For businesses with a strong review presence on Google/Yelp, Trustindex provides a convenient display layer.

---

## The Limitation: You're Dependent on What You Already Have

Trustindex aggregates. It doesn't collect. If you want to get new testimonials from happy clients, Trustindex doesn't help with that workflow.

**The dependency problem:**
- If you have few or no Google reviews, the widget is nearly empty
- External platform reviews are often short (3–4 words) rather than detailed stories
- You can't ask for specific story formats ("tell me about a specific result you got")
- Platform reviews can be removed or flagged at any time, outside your control

## They Can Work Together

Trustindex and SocialProof solve different parts of the problem. Trustindex shows what you've collected on external platforms. SocialProof helps you collect richer, more compelling testimonials directly from clients — especially when you want detailed stories or specific outcomes highlighted.

For many small businesses, starting with SocialProof to build a curated testimonial library makes the most sense. Once you have 20–30 great testimonials, adding a Trustindex widget for your Google reviews provides a complementary layer.
