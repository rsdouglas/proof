# How Online Coaches Get Testimonials That Sell Programs Without Sales Calls

Online coaching is sold on transformation. You're not selling hours or deliverables — you're selling a version of someone's life that doesn't exist yet. The prospects who need the most convincing are the ones who most need your help.

Testimonials from people who've been where your prospects are — and gotten to where they want to be — are how you close without a hard sell.

## The Online Coach's Sales Problem

You can't shake hands. You can't read the room. You're selling at scale to people who've never met you and are skeptical of every coach they've encountered. The credibility gap is real.

Strong transformation testimonials bridge that gap. When someone reads a story from a person exactly like them — same situation, same fears, same starting point — and sees where that person ended up, your sales page does the work.

## The "Before / During / After" Framework

Coach testimonials convert best in three parts:

1. **Before** — where the client was when they started. What their life looked like, what they'd tried, why they were stuck.
2. **During** — what the coaching experience was like. What you taught them, how you challenged them, what shifted.
3. **After** — specific, measurable outcomes. What changed. What their life looks like now.

Prompt clients explicitly for all three.

## When to Ask

**At program completion** — the graduation moment. Transformation is freshest, gratitude is highest.

**At a major milestone within the program** — first client signed, first $10K month, lost 20 lbs, relationship breakthrough.

**3 months after completion** — when the changes have stuck and clients are living proof.

## Scripts for Online Coaches

**Program completion:**
> "Congratulations — you've done the work and the results are real! Before we wrap, I'd love to ask one favor: would you be willing to share a testimonial about your experience and transformation?
>
> The most powerful testimonials include: where you were before, what shifted during our work together, and where you are now — with any specific results you can share. [link]
>
> Your story could be the thing that convinces someone who's exactly where you were to finally take the leap."

**Milestone moment:**
> "This is exactly why I do this work — this moment right here. Would you capture this in a short testimonial? I want other people in your position to know this is possible for them too. [link]"

## What a Converting Online Coach Testimonial Looks Like

**Weak:** "Great coach, very supportive and knowledgeable."

**Strong:** "I came to [coach] broke, burnt out, and charging $50/hr as a freelancer because I didn't believe I was worth more. Six months later, I've repackaged my services into a $5K offer, signed 4 clients at that rate, and had my first $20K month. More than the money — I finally believe I can build the business I want. [Coach] didn't just teach me strategy; she dismantled every story I had about why I couldn't."

Before (broke, undercharging, disbelieving) + specific numbers ($5K offer, 4 clients, $20K month) + mindset shift = a testimonial that sells the whole person.

## Video Testimonials for Coaches

Video converts at 2-3x the rate of text for online coaches, because it proves the transformation is real and the person is genuine. Ask your most enthusiastic graduates for a 2-minute Loom video.

## Where to Display Coach Testimonials

- **Sales page** — multiple testimonials, organized by type of transformation
- **Instagram Stories and Highlights** — "Client Wins" highlight is essential
- **Email welcome sequence** — include testimonials in onboarding emails
- **Webinar slides** — open with transformation stories before pitching
- **YouTube / podcast** — feature client success stories
