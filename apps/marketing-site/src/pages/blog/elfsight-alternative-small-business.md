# Looking for an Elfsight Alternative? Here's a Free Option for Small Businesses

Elfsight is one of the most popular widget platforms on the internet. Millions of small business owners use it to add review sliders, testimonial carousels, Instagram feeds, and countdown timers to their websites.

If you're reading this, you're probably paying $15–$60/month for Elfsight — or you just hit the plan limit and the pricing page made you wince.

Here's the thing: if all you need is a **testimonial widget**, you don't need to pay for an all-in-one widget platform. You can get it free.

---

## Why People Look for an Elfsight Alternative

Elfsight is genuinely useful if you need 20 different widget types. But most small businesses use it for one or two things. The most common use case? **Displaying customer testimonials**.

The problem is, Elfsight charges for the whole platform — not for individual widgets. So you end up paying $15/month on the Lite plan just to keep your testimonials visible, even if that's the only thing you're using it for.

Other common pain points:
- **The testimonials are static.** You manually add them inside Elfsight's editor. There's no way to send a collection link and have customers fill out a form.
- **No email collection links.** If you want to ask a customer for a testimonial, you're still doing it manually, copying and pasting their words, then uploading the text into Elfsight.
- **Pricing scales by views.** On the Lite plan, your widget stops loading after 200 views/month. For any business with real traffic, that's a problem.

## What SocialProof Does Differently

[SocialProof](https://socialproof.dev) is a focused tool — it does one thing: helps you collect testimonials from customers and display them on your website.

The workflow looks like this:

1. You get a shareable collection link (like `socialproof.dev/c/your-link`)
2. You send it to happy customers via text, email, or WhatsApp
3. Customers fill out a simple form: their name, photo, and what they want to say
4. You approve it in your dashboard
5. It appears live on your website widget — no copy-paste required

The embed is one script tag. It works on Squarespace, Wix, WordPress, Webflow, Shopify — anywhere you can add custom code.

Elfsight wins if you need widgets beyond testimonials:

- Live chat widgets
- Star rating badges from Google or Yelp
