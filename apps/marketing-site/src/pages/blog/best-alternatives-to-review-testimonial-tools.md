Before we get to the comparison links, here's what we hear most often from business owners who switch:

- **Pricing jumped** — starter plans that were free suddenly cost $49–$99/month
- **Too complicated** — features built for enterprise teams they'll never use
- **No website widget** — tools that collect reviews but can't display them on your site
- **Platform lock-in** — can't export testimonials or use them outside the tool's ecosystem
- **Review-gating issues** — some tools have been penalized by Google for non-compliant practices

SocialProof fixes all of these: it's **free forever for 1 active widget**, built for non-technical users, and your testimonials are portable.
