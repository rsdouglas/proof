# How Estheticians Get Client Testimonials That Fill Your Books

Skincare is deeply personal — and deeply trust-driven. A potential client scrolling for an esthetician isn't just looking for someone who does facials. They're looking for someone who understands *their* skin, *their* concerns, *their* goals.

Testimonials that speak to specific skin journeys are your most powerful booking tool.

## Why Esthetician Testimonials Convert

People searching for estheticians often have a specific skin concern: acne, hyperpigmentation, aging skin, sensitivity, rosacea. When they see a testimonial from someone who had their exact concern and got results, the decision is easy.

Generic "great facial, so relaxing!" testimonials are fine. Specific skin journey testimonials convert.

## When to Ask

**After visible results** — a client whose acne has cleared, whose dark spots have faded, whose skin looks noticeably different than when they started. That moment of "wow, I'm seeing it" is when to ask.

**After a series of treatments** — post-peel series, LED series, microneedling series. The investment was larger, the results should be visible.

**When a client compliments their own skin in the mirror** — they're looking at their skin glowing post-facial and they say "wow." That's your moment.

**At rebooking** — "I'd love to ask you a quick favor before you go..."

## Scripts for Estheticians

**In person (post-treatment):**
> "Your skin looks incredible — I love seeing that glow. Would you be willing to leave a quick review or testimonial? Even just a few sentences about your skin before and after, and what the experience was like. I'll send you a link right now."

**Text follow-up (24 hours post-facial):**
> "Hi [Name]! Hope your skin is still glowing today 🌟 If you have 2 minutes, I'd love a short testimonial from you — especially if you want to mention your skin concern and how it's improved. Here's the link: [link] Thanks!"

**Email for series clients:**
> Subject: Your skin transformation 💫
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> We've been on quite a skin journey together — and look at where you are now! Your [acne/hyperpigmentation/etc.] has changed so much since your first appointment.
>
> Would you be willing to share your experience in a short testimonial? Your story could help someone else who's struggling with the same thing. [link]
>
> Thank you,
> [Your name]

## What a Great Esthetician Testimonial Looks Like

**Weak:** "Nice facials, good products, clean studio."

**Strong:** "I've struggled with cystic acne for 10 years and had basically given up. After 4 sessions of [treatment] with [esthetician], my skin is clearer than it's been since I was a teenager. She figured out what my skin actually needed instead of just throwing products at it. I'm not exaggerating when I say she changed my relationship with my face."

Specific condition + duration + treatment + before/after + emotional payoff = booking magnet.

## Skin Transformation Photos

If clients are open to it, before/after photos with testimonials are enormously powerful for estheticians. Always get explicit written consent and be transparent about editing (or lack thereof).

## Where to Display Esthetician Testimonials

- **Booking page / website** — directly adjacent to the book now button
- **Google Business Profile** — "esthetician near me" + "facial near me" search
- **Instagram Highlights** — create a "Skin Stories" highlight reel
- **Vagaro, StyleSeat, or your booking platform** — many have review features
