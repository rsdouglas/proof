# Cloutly Alternative for Small Business: Testimonial Widgets Without the Complexity

Cloutly is a review generation platform that helps businesses gather and manage reviews across multiple platforms — Google, Facebook, and others. If you're a local business trying to drive up your Google star count, Cloutly is designed for that.

But if you're a service business, coach, or freelancer whose clients don't primarily find you through Google Maps, the multi-platform review funnel may be more than you need.

---

## The Use Case Difference

Cloutly is built around *review generation* — moving customers toward leaving public reviews on third-party platforms. It's an acquisition and reputation tool.

SocialProof is built around *testimonial collection* — capturing what your clients say about you, in their own words, and displaying that on your website where buyers are making decisions. It's a conversion tool.

These are different jobs. One lives before the sale (building public credibility), the other lives at the point of sale (helping prospects commit).
