# Kudobuzz Alternative: Simpler Testimonial Collection for Small Business

Kudobuzz is a social commerce and review aggregation platform that pulls reviews from Facebook, Google, Etsy, and Amazon into a unified display widget for e-commerce stores.

If you run an online store and want to surface reviews from multiple channels, Kudobuzz offers a consolidated view. But for service businesses — coaches, consultants, therapists, freelancers — social commerce features aren't the point.

---

## Built for Products, Not Services

Kudobuzz's primary use case is showing product reviews from multiple marketplaces to build confidence in e-commerce purchases. The social media integrations, star ratings, and product-linked reviews are all designed around "should I buy this product?"

Service businesses need a different answer: "Should I hire this person?" That question requires testimonials — stories about outcomes, transformations, and results. SocialProof is built to collect and display exactly that.
