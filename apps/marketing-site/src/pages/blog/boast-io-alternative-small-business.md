Boast.io has been around since 2012 and is well-known in the testimonial and review collection space. But for many small business owners, it's more tool than they need — and the pricing reflects that.

If you're looking for a Boast.io alternative that's simpler, faster to set up, and free to start, here's an honest comparison.

## What Is Boast.io?

Boast.io is a testimonial collection platform that lets businesses collect written testimonials, video testimonials, and NPS surveys. It's used by a range of businesses from solo coaches to enterprise teams.

Their key features include:
- Request forms for testimonials
- Video testimonial capture
- Automated follow-up sequences
- CRM integrations
- Embeddable widgets

It's a solid platform — but it's priced and designed for teams, not solo operators and small businesses.

## Boast.io Pricing

Boast.io's pricing starts around $25/month for their base plan, with higher tiers for more features and responses. For a small business collecting testimonials occasionally, this can feel like overkill.

There's a free trial, but no permanent free plan.

## Why Small Businesses Look for Alternatives

The most common reasons we hear from small business owners who switched away from Boast:

**"I just needed something simple."** Boast's interface has a learning curve. For a business owner who wants to send a link to a happy customer and get a testimonial, the setup friction is real.

**"I don't need video."** Video testimonials are powerful, but most small businesses — coaches, consultants, salons, local service providers — get plenty of mileage from well-written text testimonials. Paying a premium for video collection you don't use doesn't make sense.

**"The pricing didn't match my usage."** If you're collecting 10–20 testimonials per year, you don't need enterprise-tier features.

**"I wanted a widget I could actually embed."** Some users reported friction getting the embed to work cleanly on their specific platform (Squarespace, Wix, etc.).
