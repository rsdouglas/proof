# Reviewshake Alternative for Small Business: Testimonial Collection Without the Platform Tax

Reviewshake is a review management platform that helps businesses generate reviews on Google, Facebook, and other platforms, monitor incoming reviews, and respond to them. It's designed for local businesses and agencies managing multiple clients.

The platform offers white-label options, multi-location dashboards, and review generation campaigns — features that are valuable at agency scale, but more than most independent service providers need.

---

## Agency Scale vs. Solopreneur Scale

Reviewshake's pricing and feature set are calibrated for agencies managing 10–100+ locations. If you're an individual service provider managing one business, you're paying for infrastructure you won't use.

SocialProof is built for one-person shops and small service businesses. The whole tool is focused on the core workflow: send a testimonial request, receive client response, approve it, display it on your site.
