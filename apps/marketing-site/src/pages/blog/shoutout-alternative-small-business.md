# Shoutout Alternative for Service Businesses: Testimonials Without the Video Requirement

Shoutout is a platform for collecting and displaying video testimonials. The idea is compelling — video adds credibility and personality to social proof. Clients film a short clip on their phone, and you embed it on your website.

There's a real friction problem: most clients won't do it. Asking someone to appear on camera, record themselves, and submit a video is a much higher bar than asking them to write a few sentences. The result? You ask ten clients, maybe one or two come through.

---

## Reducing Friction Increases Conversion

The best testimonial is the one you actually have. Written testimonials have a higher completion rate because they're easier to do. A client can respond to a testimonial request link during a commute or in a spare five minutes. Recording video requires preparation, lighting, and comfort on camera.

SocialProof focuses on written testimonials for exactly this reason: lower friction, more responses, more social proof on your website faster.
