Mobile notaries and loan signing agents operate in a high-volume, fast-turnaround market where Google Maps is the primary discovery channel and reviews are the primary trust signal.

A notary with 200 Google reviews showing up in the map pack will book more appointments than one with a polished website and 12 reviews. Volume and recency of reviews matters enormously.

## Who Is Booking Mobile Notaries

- Real estate buyers and sellers completing loan signings
- Attorneys needing client documents notarized
- Individuals with healthcare directives, wills, or powers of attorney
- Businesses handling contracts and HR documents
- People needing I-9 remote authorization
- Travelers who need documents notarized on short notice

## The Review Timing Formula

Mobile notary appointments are quick. The best time to ask for a review is immediately after — while the client is still satisfied and you're still top of mind.

**Text message 30 minutes after the appointment:**
> "Thanks for choosing [your name] today! If I made things easy, a quick Google review would mean a lot: [link] — takes 60 seconds and helps people find reliable notary service."

**Follow-up for loan signings:**
> "The signing is complete! If the experience was smooth, I'd really appreciate a Google review — it's how people find me when they need a notary fast: [link]"

## What a Strong Notary Review Looks Like

*"Needed a mobile notary for my dad's power of attorney documents — time sensitive and he's not mobile. [Name] came to my dad's house in 45 minutes flat, was patient and clear explaining what he was signing, and handled everything professionally. I'll never use anyone else. If you need a notary fast, call her."*

Strong elements: urgent situation solved, speed of response, patience with an elderly client, a clear endorsement, and future intent.

## Volume Is Your Metric

Unlike a massage therapist or an attorney, a mobile notary does 5-10 appointments a day. At that volume, you should be generating 50+ reviews per month if you ask consistently.

Goal: 500+ Google reviews within your first year of asking. This level of review volume creates a near-insurmountable advantage in local search.
