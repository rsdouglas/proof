Pilates clients are among the most loyal in the fitness industry. They don't just work out — they develop a relationship with their studio, their instructors, and a method that changes how they feel in their body. That loyalty is your most powerful marketing asset.

A prospective client who reads a heartfelt testimonial from someone who's been coming for three years and finally has a pain-free back — they're already sold before they set foot in your studio.

## Who Is Your New Client

- Someone who's been told by a PT to try Pilates for back pain, hip issues, or post-partum recovery
- A yoga or barre practitioner who wants to explore Pilates
- An athlete (dancer, runner, golfer) looking for cross-training or injury prevention
- Someone who tried Pilates once and is looking for a better studio
- A new mom looking to reconnect with her body safely

Each of these clients responds differently to testimonials. Segmenting your stories by client type dramatically increases conversion.

## When to Ask

**After the first month:** New clients who survive the first month become long-term members at much higher rates. Check in personally:
> "It's been about a month since you started — how are you feeling? Any changes in your body? ...That's wonderful. Would you be willing to share that in a quick review or testimonial? It would help other people who are exactly where you were a month ago: [link]"

**When a client shares a win:** Milestone moments are gold — a client mentions their back hasn't hurt in weeks, they ran their first marathon, they wore something they couldn't before:
> "That's exactly why I do this. Would you put that in writing? A sentence or two means so much to someone considering their first class: [link]"

**At the 1-year anniversary:** Long-term clients have transformation stories. Reach out personally:
> "Can you believe it's been a year? I've watched you completely transform. Would you share your story? Other people considering Pilates really need to hear what's possible: [link]"

## The Most Powerful Pilates Testimonials

**The pain story:**
*"I had chronic lower back pain for seven years. I'd tried everything — PT, chiropractic, acupuncture. [Studio name] was the first thing that actually made a lasting difference. Six months in, I'm largely pain-free. My PT asked what I was doing differently. I told her: Pilates, twice a week."*

**The body change story:**
*"I didn't come to Pilates to lose weight — I came to stop hurting. But after eight months, my posture is completely different, I'm three inches taller when I stand, and I fit into jeans I haven't worn in years. My friends keep asking what I'm doing."*

**The return to movement story:**
*"I was scared to exercise after my C-section. [Instructor name] met me exactly where I was and helped me rebuild from the inside out. I feel stronger than I did before I was pregnant."*

## Video Testimonials

Pilates transformations are visual. Short video testimonials — even 30 seconds on an iPhone — showing someone moving fluidly who once couldn't are incredibly compelling. Ask long-term clients who are regulars and will shine on camera.
