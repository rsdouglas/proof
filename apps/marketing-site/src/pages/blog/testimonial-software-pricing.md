# Testimonial Software Pricing: What Does It Actually Cost?

If you're shopping for testimonial software, you've probably noticed prices range from free to several hundred dollars a month. What accounts for the difference, and what do you actually need?

Here's a breakdown of what's typically available at each price point.

## What Testimonial Software Does

At its core, testimonial software solves three problems:

1. **Collection** — a link or form customers use to submit their testimonial
2. **Moderation** — approve what goes live, reject what doesn't
3. **Display** — show approved testimonials on your website via embed or widget

Some tools add features like rich media (video testimonials), CRM integrations, automated review requests, and custom branding. Whether those features are worth paying for depends on your business.

## Typical Price Ranges

### Free plans

**What you get:** Usually basic collection and a simple embed widget. Often limited to 1 widget or a handful of testimonials. May include branding from the platform ("Powered by X").

**Best for:** Solopreneurs, freelancers, early-stage startups. If you need to collect and display a handful of testimonials on one site, free is usually enough to start.

**SocialProof free plan:** Free forever for 1 active widget. Unlimited testimonial submissions, full approval flow, embeds on any website. No credit card required.

### $10–25/month

**What you get:** Multiple widgets, custom branding removal, more testimonials, some automation (auto-request emails after a transaction).

**Best for:** Small businesses with 2–5 locations or brands, or agencies managing testimonials for multiple clients.

### $50–100/month

**What you get:** Video testimonials, rich media uploads, deeper integrations (Zapier, CRM, email marketing platforms), team seats, advanced analytics.

**Best for:** Growing businesses running active review campaigns, SaaS companies using testimonials in sales cycles, marketing teams.

### $200–500+/month

**What you get:** Enterprise features — white-label, SSO, bulk API access, dedicated support, compliance features.

**Best for:** Agencies managing dozens of client accounts, large e-commerce brands, B2B companies with complex use cases.

## What Most Small Businesses Actually Need

For the majority of small business owners, freelancers, and solopreneurs:

- One collection link to share with customers
- Approval control before anything goes live
- An embed that works on their website platform (Squarespace, Webflow, Wix, Shopify, etc.)

That's it. You don't need video testimonials when you're starting out. You don't need Zapier integrations. You don't need a team inbox.

A free or low-cost plan covers this entirely.

## What to Watch Out For

**Branding on embeds:** Some free plans watermark your testimonial widget with their logo. If you're building a professional brand, this can undermine the credibility you're trying to build.

**Testimonial limits:** Some plans charge per testimonial submission, which punishes success. Look for plans with unlimited collection even if you pay for the display features.

**Platform lock-in:** If your testimonials are stored in a proprietary format with no export, you could lose access if the tool shuts down or you switch providers. Check for CSV or JSON export.

**Upsell pressure:** Some tools start free but require upgrading to do anything useful (embed, share, approve). Read the full feature list before committing to a workflow around a platform.
