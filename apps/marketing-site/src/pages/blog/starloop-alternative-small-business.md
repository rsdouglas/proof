# Starloop Alternative: Collect Testimonials Without the Review Gating Concerns

Starloop is a review generation tool that sends automated messages asking customers to leave reviews on Google and Facebook. The platform is built around increasing star ratings on third-party platforms — it's a review solicitation engine.

If your goal is to boost your Google star rating, Starloop addresses that. If your goal is to own a collection of client testimonials and display them on your website, the third-party platform dependency is a vulnerability, not a feature.

---

## Platform Dependency vs. Ownership

When your social proof lives on Google and Facebook, you're dependent on those platforms' moderation decisions. Reviews can disappear. Algorithms change. You can't control how your testimonials are displayed or which ones get featured.

With SocialProof, your testimonials live on your website. You collect them, approve them, and decide how they're displayed. No platform can remove them. No algorithm buries them.
