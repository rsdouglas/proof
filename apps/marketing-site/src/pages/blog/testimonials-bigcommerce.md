BigCommerce comes with solid product review functionality out of the box. But there's a gap most merchants miss: **testimonials aren't product reviews**.

Testimonials are customer stories about your brand — the transformation, the experience, the result. They live on your homepage, your about page, and your checkout page. They're the words that make a first-time visitor say "okay, I trust this place."

This guide shows you how to add testimonials to BigCommerce without touching your theme code.

---

## Why product reviews aren't enough

BigCommerce's built-in reviews are product-specific. A five-star review on your best-selling item is great — but it doesn't build overall brand trust the way a testimonial does.

The difference:

**Product review:** "The blue denim jacket is exactly as described, fast shipping ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐"

**Testimonial:** "I was nervous ordering online because I'd had bad experiences before. But the sizing guide was accurate, it arrived in 2 days, and the quality is incredible. I've ordered three more times since."

The testimonial tells a story. It overcomes the objections a new visitor has. It converts.

---

## What most BigCommerce stores get wrong

Most stores either:

1. **Display nothing** — they rely on product reviews buried on product pages
2. **Screenshot Google reviews** — blurry, unstyled, and impossible to update
3. **Hardcode text in their theme** — which breaks every time they update

None of these scale. None of them look professional. And none of them are easy to manage.

## Where to display testimonials on BigCommerce

The three highest-converting spots:

**1. Homepage** — First impression trust. Use 2–4 strong testimonials above the fold or just below your hero banner.

**2. Cart/Checkout page** — Customers who are on the fence need reassurance. A testimonial here can recover abandoned carts.

**3. About page** — Especially valuable for smaller brands where "who is this company?" is the primary trust concern.

---

## How to collect your first testimonials

Most merchants have happy customers who've never been asked. Here's the fastest approach:

1. **Email your last 10–20 customers** — "Quick favor: can you share a sentence about your experience? Here's the link." Response rates are surprisingly high (20–40%) when you ask directly.

2. **Add the link to your order confirmation email** — BigCommerce lets you customize this. Adding "Loved your order? Share your experience →" with your collection link converts at 5–10% passively.

3. **Add it to your post-purchase follow-up** — If you use email automation (Klaviyo, Mailchimp, etc.), add your testimonial request to the 7-day post-purchase flow.
