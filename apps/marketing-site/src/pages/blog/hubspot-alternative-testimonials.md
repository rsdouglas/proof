# HubSpot Alternative for Testimonials: Why Small Businesses Don't Need a CRM to Collect Social Proof

HubSpot is the dominant CRM and marketing platform for B2B companies. It has forms, surveys, feedback tools, email automation, and about 200 other features. If your company has a dedicated sales team and pays $800+/month for the full suite, it's powerful.

But many small businesses try to use HubSpot forms or feedback surveys to collect customer testimonials — and it's overkill. Expensive overkill.

---

## What HubSpot Offers for Testimonials

HubSpot doesn't have a dedicated testimonial tool. What people use instead:

- **HubSpot Forms** — create a form asking for testimonial text
- **Customer Feedback Surveys** — NPS or CSAT surveys that include open-ended feedback
- **Service Hub** — includes customer feedback features at the $450+/month tier

The problem: none of these are designed to collect and display testimonials. You collect the data, then you're on your own.

---

## The Real Cost of Using HubSpot for Testimonials

| HubSpot Tier | Monthly Cost | Testimonial Features |
|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | Basic forms only, no display |
| Starter | $20/month | Forms + email, no testimonial widget |
| Professional | $450+/month | Feedback surveys, still no display widget |

And HubSpot's free form responses are limited. You need paid tiers to get automation, sequences, and anything beyond basic collection.
