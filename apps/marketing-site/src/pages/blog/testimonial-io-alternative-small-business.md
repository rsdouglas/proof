# Testimonial.io Alternative: Same Core Features, Half the Price

Testimonial.io is one of the best-known testimonial tools in the market. It pioneered the idea of a simple collection link + embeddable wall of love, and a lot of indie hackers and SaaS founders swear by it.

The core product is solid. But at $50/month for the features most small businesses actually need (unlimited testimonials, custom domains, video), the price point is hard to justify for a local service business, freelancer, or solopreneur.

---

## What Testimonial.io Does Well

- **Wall of love widget** — a beautiful scrolling grid of testimonials
- **Video testimonials** — guided recording in the browser
- **Custom collection page** — branded form at your-name.testimonial.to
- **Third-party imports** — pull in reviews from Product Hunt, G2, etc.
- **Embeds** — works on any website

If you're a funded SaaS company trying to build social proof at scale, Testimonial.io is worth the $50/month.

---

## Where It Gets Expensive for Small Business

Testimonial.io's free plan is very limited. The features most small businesses need — video testimonials, custom branding, unlimited testimonials, multiple widgets — require the $50/month Starter plan or the $150/month Growth plan.

For a freelance photographer, local accountant, or independent therapist, that's a real number. Especially when the tool is used primarily to display testimonials on one website.
