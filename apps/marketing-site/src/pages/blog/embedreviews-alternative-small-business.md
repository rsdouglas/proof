# EmbedReviews Alternative: Display Your Own Testimonials Instead of Aggregating Others

EmbedReviews is a platform for pulling in reviews from Google, Yelp, Facebook, and other sources and displaying them on your website. If you have a strong external review presence, it's a useful aggregation tool.
