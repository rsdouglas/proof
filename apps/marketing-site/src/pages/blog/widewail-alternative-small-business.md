# Widewail Alternative for Small Business: Managed Review Services vs. DIY Testimonials

Widewail is a managed reputation and review response service that deploys human experts to respond to your reviews on Google, Facebook, and other platforms on your behalf. It's designed for automotive dealerships, healthcare networks, and enterprise businesses managing high review volumes.

Widewail isn't software you run yourself — it's a service. The team monitors your reviews and crafts responses for you. That managed service model comes with managed service pricing.

---

## Managed Service vs. Simple Tool

If you're running a business that receives hundreds of reviews a month across dozens of locations, outsourcing the response function makes sense. If you're a solo practitioner or small service business, you can respond to your own reviews — and you probably should, because it's also client relationship work.

What you actually need isn't a managed response team. It's a simple way to collect testimonials from happy clients and display them where prospects will see them. SocialProof handles that workflow in five minutes.
