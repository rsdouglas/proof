# Reviews.io Alternative for Small Business: Is $89/Month Worth It?

Reviews.io is a serious review management platform used by medium and large businesses. It aggregates reviews from Google, Trustpilot, and other platforms, sends automated email/SMS review requests, and has robust reporting.

If you're a business with 1,000+ customers per month and a dedicated marketing manager, it might be worth it. If you're a small business owner doing everything yourself, probably not.

---

## What Reviews.io Offers

Reviews.io is built for businesses that:
- Want to aggregate reviews from multiple platforms in one dashboard
- Need to respond to reviews at scale
- Send automated SMS and email review request sequences
- Want verified reviews for Google Shopping ads
- Have a team managing customer feedback

The feature set is genuinely comprehensive. The price reflects that.

---

## Reviews.io Pricing for Small Business

Reviews.io's plans start around $89/month for small plans and go up to $200+/month for more features. Annual plans bring the price down, but you're still looking at $1,000+/year minimum.

For a small business generating $5,000–$20,000/month in revenue, that's a meaningful percentage of profit going to a review platform.

---

## What Most Small Businesses Actually Need

Let's be honest about what most small business owners want from a review tool:

1. Ask happy customers to leave a testimonial
2. Display those testimonials on their website
3. Have the process be simple and not require a marketing manager

That's it. That's the 80% case.
