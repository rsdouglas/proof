# Testimonials for Tree Service Companies: Build Trust Before the Truck Shows Up

A homeowner calling a tree service company is worried. A 40-foot oak hanging over their roof. A dead tree that could fall on the neighbor's fence. They need the job done safely, cleanly, and without drama — and they're going to hire someone they trust.

That trust gets built before you ever show up for an estimate. It's built by testimonials.

## Why Tree Service Testimonials Hit Differently

Tree work is uniquely anxiety-inducing as a purchase:

- **High property risk** — mistakes mean damage to homes, fences, power lines
- **Difficult to evaluate skill** — homeowners can't assess your technique from an estimate
- **Large ticket size** — $1,500–$8,000 jobs require serious confidence before signing
- **Insurance concerns** — uninsured crews create homeowner liability

Testimonials directly address these fears. When someone reads "They took down a 60-foot silver maple 8 feet from my house and didn't scratch the siding" — that does more work than any marketing copy you could write.

## What Great Tree Service Testimonials Include

The most effective testimonials for tree companies typically mention:

**The specific job:** "They removed three large oaks and ground all three stumps..."

**Safety or precision:** "...two of them were very close to our fence and they got them down clean."

**Cleanup:** "...the yard was cleaner after they left than before they arrived."

**Professionalism:** "...the crew was respectful, explained exactly what they were doing, and didn't leave until I was happy."

**The result:** "...we've hired them twice now and recommend them to everyone in the neighborhood."

## Questions to Ask After a Tree Job

Send this after completing the work:

1. What job did we do for you? (removal, trimming, stump grinding, emergency work?)
2. Was there anything that made the job tricky — location, size, access?
3. How did the crew handle the job?
4. What did your yard look like when we were done?
5. Anything that stood out or surprised you about working with us?

## Where to Use Testimonials

**Homepage:** "What Customers Say" section with 4–6 recent quotes. Lead with dramatic examples ("removed 80-foot oak, zero damage").

**Services pages:** Match testimonials to services. Stump grinding page gets stump grinding testimonials. Emergency tree removal page gets emergency response testimonials.

**Before/after photos + quotes:** Post on Google Business Profile, Facebook, Nextdoor. "Before" (dangerous tree) + "After" (clean yard) + customer quote = extremely shareable content.

**Google reviews:** Actively request Google reviews alongside your own testimonial widget. Google reviews influence local search rankings; your own widget gives you complete control over display.

**Nextdoor + neighborhood Facebook groups:** Tree emergencies are hyper-local. When a homeowner posts "anyone have a tree service recommendation?" in a neighborhood group, your team can respond with a link to your testimonials page.

## Building a Consistent Testimonial System

The busiest tree companies don't remember to ask. Build a system:

1. **After every job**, have your crew lead or office send a text: "Thanks for trusting us with your tree! If you're happy with how it went, a quick review would mean a lot: [your SocialProof link]. Takes 2 minutes."

2. **Create a printed card** with a QR code linking to your collection page. Hand it to the homeowner when you collect payment.

3. **Route the best testimonials** automatically to your website widget.
