# JotForm Alternative for Collecting Testimonials: Go Beyond Form Responses

JotForm is one of the most popular form builders in the world. Drag-and-drop builder, 10,000+ templates, powerful integrations. For general form needs, it's hard to beat.

But when small business owners use JotForm to collect testimonials, they hit the same wall every time: great at collecting, useless at displaying.

---

## How People Use JotForm for Testimonials

The common workflow:
1. Build a JotForm with fields for name, role, and testimonial text
2. Share the link with customers
3. Responses pile up in the JotForm dashboard
4. ... manually copy them to your website
5. ... or forget entirely

The collection step works fine. But JotForm has no way to automatically display those testimonials on your website. Every update is manual.

---

## The JotForm Testimonial Gap

### No Testimonial Widget

JotForm can collect testimonials. It cannot publish them. There's no embed code that shows "testimonials collected via this form" on your website. You have to export, format, and manually update your site — every time.

### Limited Free Tier

JotForm's free plan allows 5 forms and 100 monthly submissions. For a growing business sending testimonial requests to every new customer, that cap is tight.

### No Video Testimonials

JotForm supports file uploads, but not purpose-built video testimonial collection with guided prompts and easy customer recording.
