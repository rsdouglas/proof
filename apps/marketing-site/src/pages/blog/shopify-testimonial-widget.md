When the widget is configured how you want it, SocialProof gives you a small snippet of HTML — two lines. That's what you'll add to Shopify.


## Step 3: Add the Widget to Your Shopify Store


Here's where most guides get complicated. They don't have to be.


### Option A: Add to a page using a Custom HTML block (easiest)


This works for any Shopify page — your homepage, About page, a campaign landing page, or a standalone testimonials page.


1. In your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store → Themes**
1. Click **Customize** on your active theme
1. Navigate to the page where you want the testimonials to appear
1. Click **Add section** (or **Add block** within an existing section)
1. Choose **Custom HTML** or **Custom Liquid** — both work
1. Paste your SocialProof widget code into the field
1. Click **Save**


That's it. The widget loads live on your page. If you update which testimonials are shown in your SocialProof dashboard, the widget on your site updates automatically — no re-editing your theme.


### Option B: Add to a product page


If you want testimonials specifically on product pages (separate from Shopify's built-in review stars), you can add a Custom HTML section to your product page template:


1. In Customize, navigate to any product page
1. In the left sidebar, find your product page sections
1. Click **Add block** within the product information section, or **Add section** below it
1. Choose **Custom HTML**
1. Paste your widget code
1. Save


This will show the same testimonial widget on all product pages. If you want different testimonials per product, you can create multiple SocialProof widgets — one per product or collection — and use Shopify's template system to assign them. (This gets into Liquid templates and is a one-time developer task if you need it.)


### Option C: Add to your cart or checkout pages


Shopify restricts what you can embed on native checkout pages (you need Shopify Plus for full customization). But you *can* add testimonials to:


- Your **cart page** (before the customer hits Checkout)
- Any **page-type** you've created in Online Store → Pages
- Your **thank you / order confirmation page** via Shopify's "Additional scripts" field (Settings → Checkout)


A testimonial on the cart page is particularly powerful — it's one of the last chances to reinforce trust before someone abandons.

## Common Questions


### Will this slow down my store?


SocialProof widgets are lightweight. The script is small and loads asynchronously — it won't block your page render or hurt your Core Web Vitals. Shopify themes already load many more kilobytes of JavaScript from their own apps.


### Can I show different testimonials on different pages?


Yes. Create multiple widgets in your SocialProof dashboard — each with its own selection of testimonials — and paste the corresponding widget code wherever you want it.


### What if I don't have any testimonials yet?


Start by sharing your SocialProof collection link with your most recent customers. You need zero testimonials before you sign up — the collection link exists the moment your account does. Five well-written testimonials will outperform fifty generic ones.


### Do customers need to log into anything?


No. The collection link opens a simple, hosted form. Your customers don't create an account or install anything — they just fill out the form and submit. That's why completion rates are high.


### Can I moderate which testimonials appear?


Yes. Every testimonial submitted through your collection link goes into your SocialProof dashboard for approval before it appears on your site. You approve and curate — nothing shows up automatically.
