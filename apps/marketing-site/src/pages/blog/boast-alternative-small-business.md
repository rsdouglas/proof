# Boast Alternative for Small Business: Simpler Testimonial Collection Without the Price Tag

Boast is a dedicated testimonial platform that has been around since 2012. It offers testimonial collection via custom forms, response management, and embedding options for your website.

The core idea is the same as SocialProof: collect testimonials from happy clients and show them off. The main difference is pricing and what that pricing unlocks.

---

## What Boast Offers

- Testimonial collection forms (text and video response)
- Automated collection campaigns
- Testimonial management dashboard
- Website embeds and display widgets
- Integrations with CRMs and email tools

---

## The Pricing Gap

Boast's pricing starts around $49/month for the basic plan, with team and enterprise tiers going higher. For solo operators and small service businesses watching every dollar, that's a meaningful monthly commitment before you've collected a single testimonial.

SocialProof offers a free forever plan that covers a single widget — enough to get started, test whether clients respond, and build your first handful of testimonials without any financial risk.
