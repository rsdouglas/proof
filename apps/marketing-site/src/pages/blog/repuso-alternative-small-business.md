# Repuso Alternative for Small Business: Own Your Testimonials

Repuso is a testimonials and social proof platform that aggregates user-generated content from Twitter, Facebook, email, and other sources into a display widget for your website.

The aggregation approach works well if you have an active social media presence where customers mention you organically. For many service businesses, that's not the reality.

---

## The Social Mention Problem

Repuso's value proposition depends on people talking about you on social media. If you're a coach, consultant, or service professional, your best client feedback happens in private conversations, email exchanges, or off-platform entirely.

SocialProof provides a direct collection link — share it with a client and they respond in a structured form. No social media account required. No waiting for mentions. You actively create the testimonial content rather than scraping for it.
