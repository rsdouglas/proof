# Reviewflowz Alternative: Testimonial Collection vs. Review Notification Monitoring

Reviewflowz is a review monitoring and notification tool. When someone leaves a review on Google, Yelp, G2, Capterra, or other platforms, Reviewflowz pings your Slack channel with the new review. It also has basic review collection campaigns.

If you're a SaaS company tracking reviews across G2, Capterra, and Trustpilot, Reviewflowz makes sense as a monitoring layer. For service businesses who need to proactively collect testimonials from clients, it's the wrong starting point.

---

## Monitoring vs. Collecting

There's a meaningful difference between monitoring for reviews that happen to come in and actively soliciting testimonials from your best clients.

Reviewflowz is a monitoring tool — it tells you when the thing happened. SocialProof is a collection tool — it helps you make the thing happen. If you wait for organic reviews, you might wait a long time. If you ask clients directly with a purpose-built workflow, you get testimonials this week.
