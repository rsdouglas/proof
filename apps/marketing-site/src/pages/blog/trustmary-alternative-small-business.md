# Trustmary Alternative for Small Business: Powerful Software, Simpler Price Point

Trustmary is one of the most thoughtfully designed testimonial platforms on the market. Beautiful video testimonial collection, NPS surveys, conversion-focused widgets, and solid analytics. If you've researched testimonial software, you've probably encountered it.

But Trustmary's pricing starts at around $19/month for a very limited free plan and goes to $89+/month for full features. For a solopreneur or small business owner, that's a significant ongoing expense for social proof.

---

## What Trustmary Does Well

Trustmary genuinely excels at:

- **Video testimonial collection** — the customer recording experience is smooth and guided
- **NPS and CSAT surveys** — integrated feedback collection beyond just testimonials
- **A/B testing widgets** — test different testimonial displays to see what converts
- **Analytics** — track widget views, clicks, and conversion impact
- **Lead generation forms** — combine testimonials with lead capture

If you have a mid-size business with conversion optimization as a priority and $89/month in the budget, Trustmary is worth evaluating seriously.

---

## Where Trustmary Falls Short for Small Businesses

### Price vs. Needs

Most small business owners don't need A/B testing or advanced analytics. They need: collect testimonials, display them, done. Trustmary's lower tiers limit the number of contacts and forms significantly.

### Complexity

Trustmary has a full feature suite which means a learning curve. Small business owners who want to spend 30 minutes setting up testimonials and move on may find it more than they need.
