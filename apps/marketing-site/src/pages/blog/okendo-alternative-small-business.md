# Okendo Alternative for Small Business: Shopify Reviews Tool vs. General Use

Okendo is one of the highest-rated review apps in the Shopify ecosystem. It's built specifically for Shopify product reviews — collecting star ratings, photo reviews, Q&A, and displaying them on product pages. Major DTC brands trust it, and for Shopify e-commerce, it earns its reputation.

But Okendo has two significant limitations for many small business owners.

---

## Limitation 1: It's Built Exclusively for Shopify

Okendo is a Shopify app. It doesn't work on WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, Webflow, or a custom website. If you're not running your business on Shopify, Okendo isn't available to you.

## Limitation 2: It's Built for Product Reviews, Not Service Testimonials

Okendo's entire design is around product reviews — star ratings, product attributes ("fits true to size"), photo reviews of physical items. This works beautifully for a shoe brand or skincare company.

It's not designed for:
- A therapist collecting testimonials about their practice
- A consultant asking clients what they achieved
- A photographer collecting video testimonials from wedding couples
- A coach gathering stories from transformation clients

Service businesses need **testimonials** — open-ended stories about the experience and results — not structured product review forms.
