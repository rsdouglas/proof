# Stamped.io Alternative for Small Business: Reviews vs. Testimonials

Stamped.io (now Stamped) is a reviews and loyalty platform for Shopify and e-commerce stores. It collects product reviews, net promoter scores, and Q&A, with integrations into Shopify's native review system. It's a well-regarded tool in the Shopify ecosystem.

Like most e-commerce review tools, it's built for the product purchase context: did the item arrive? Does it look like the photos? Would you recommend it?

---

## E-Commerce vs. Service Business Social Proof

Product reviews and service testimonials have different shapes. Product reviews tend to be short, often star-only, and tied to a specific purchase. Service testimonials are narrative — they describe an experience, a transformation, a working relationship.

SocialProof is designed to capture those narratives. The collection form is open-ended, encouraging clients to share their story rather than rate a product. The display widget showcases those stories in a way that builds trust in a service context.
