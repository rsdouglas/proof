# Ryviu Alternative for Small Business: Beyond E-Commerce Reviews

Ryviu is a product review and Q&A app for Shopify stores, designed to collect and display reviews on product pages. It imports reviews from AliExpress and Amazon, which is useful for dropshipping stores. The tool lives in the Shopify ecosystem and is built to serve product commerce.

If you sell physical products on Shopify, Ryviu makes sense. If you're selling a service, your expertise, or your time, you're looking for something different.

---

## Service vs. Product Social Proof

Product reviews and service testimonials serve different purposes. Product reviews answer: "Does this thing work?" Service testimonials answer: "Is this person worth hiring?" The questions are different, so the tools should be too.

SocialProof is designed for service-based businesses: consultants, coaches, therapists, agencies, freelancers. The testimonial collection form is open-ended, capturing client stories, outcomes, and transformations — not star ratings for a physical item.
