Before a seller agrees to let you list their largest asset, or a buyer agrees to trust you through the most expensive purchase of their life, they're going to check what other people say about you.

Your testimonials are doing the selling before you even walk in the door.

## What Home Buyers and Sellers Are Looking For

- Agents who communicate consistently and proactively
- Agents who know the local market deeply
- Agents who negotiate hard and protect their client's interests
- Agents who make a stressful process manageable
- Agents who deliver results (sold quickly, above asking, found the right home)

## When to Ask

**At closing:**
> "Congratulations — you've got the keys! This was a big deal and I'm so glad we got here. If you'd leave a review, it would mean everything. Takes two minutes: [link]"

**3 weeks after move-in:**
> "Hope you're settling in. If you're happy with how everything went, a Google review would help me reach other buyers in [neighborhood]: [link]"

**After a listing sells:**
> "We did it — above asking and in under two weeks. If you'd want to share what the experience was like, it helps sellers find me: [link]"

## What a Strong Real Estate Testimonial Looks Like

*"We were first-time homebuyers terrified we'd make a mistake in a competitive market. [Agent name] explained every step, warned us when a house wasn't worth the bidding war, and was available at all hours when we had questions. We got our home on our first offer — under asking — because she knew the listing had been sitting. I don't know how we would have done this without her. We've already recommended her to two coworkers."*

Strong elements: buyer context (first-time, competitive market), communication and availability, strategic advice (when NOT to bid), specific outcome (under asking, first offer), expertise signal (market knowledge), and strong referral.

## Use Testimonials at Every Conversion Point

- **Listing presentation deck** — Client testimonials from sellers
- **Buyer guide PDF** — What it's like to work with you
- **Website** — Neighborhood-specific and buyer/seller-specific testimonials
- **Email signature** — A rotating testimonial builds credibility passively
