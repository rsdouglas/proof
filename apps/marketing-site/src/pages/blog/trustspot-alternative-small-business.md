# TrustSpot Alternative for Small Business: Verified Reviews vs. Direct Testimonials

TrustSpot is a review platform that specializes in verified customer reviews, similar to Trustpilot or Reviews.io. The "verified" aspect means reviews are tied to actual purchase transactions, which helps prevent fake reviews and builds credibility for e-commerce brands.

Verification of purchase makes complete sense for product businesses. For service businesses, the verification question is different — and the answer looks different.

---

## Verification in a Service Context

In e-commerce, "verified" means "they actually bought the product." In service businesses, verification comes from the relationship itself. Your client knows you. You know them. The testimonial doesn't need to be verified by a platform — it's verified by the direct relationship.

SocialProof is built for that context: you send a testimonial request directly to a client you've worked with. Their response is inherently authentic because you chose who to ask.
