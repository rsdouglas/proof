# Reviews on My Website Alternative: Simple Testimonials for Service Businesses

Reviews on My Website is a review aggregation and display tool that pulls reviews from Google and Facebook and shows them on your website via a widget. It's a clean, straightforward tool for businesses that have an active Google or Facebook review presence.

The catch: if you don't have reviews on those platforms, the tool has nothing to show.

---

## When You Need to Create, Not Just Display

Many service businesses — especially those with referral-based client pipelines — don't rely on Google Maps or Facebook reviews. Their best client feedback lives in email, direct messages, or conversations that never make it to a public review platform.

SocialProof lets you go directly to those clients. Send a link, ask for a testimonial, receive their words, and display them on your website. You create the content rather than waiting for it to appear somewhere you can aggregate.
