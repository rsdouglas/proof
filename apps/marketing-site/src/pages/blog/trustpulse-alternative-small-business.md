# TrustPulse Alternative: Real Testimonials Beat FOMO Notifications

TrustPulse is a social proof notification tool — it shows small popups in the corner of your website saying things like "John from Austin just signed up" or "15 people are viewing this page right now."

This type of social proof works well for e-commerce stores with high traffic volume. When hundreds of people are buying per day, showing real-time activity creates genuine FOMO.

But for most small businesses, TrustPulse has a fundamental problem.

---

## The Small Business Problem With TrustPulse

TrustPulse works best when you have **actual recent activity to show**. If your service business gets 5–10 new clients per month, the notifications either:
- Show outdated activity ("Sarah signed up 3 months ago")
- Look fake because activity is sparse
- Don't fire at all on low-traffic pages

Small businesses don't need FOMO notifications. They need **real customer stories** — testimonials that explain what working with you is like, build trust for skeptical buyers, and address the questions people have before booking.

A popup saying "Maria from Portland just booked" is less convincing than Maria's actual 90-second video explaining why she chose you and what results she got.

## When to Use Each

**TrustPulse is right for you if:**
- You run an e-commerce store with 50+ purchases per day
- You have a high-traffic lead generation page
- You want to show real-time activity that actually exists

**SocialProof is right for you if:**
- You're a service business, consultant, or coach
- You want to display real customer stories and video testimonials
- You have a small but loyal customer base whose words you want to amplify
- You want trust signals that work even when you have 10 visitors per day
