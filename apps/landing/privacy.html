Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Proof ("we", "our", "us") takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

When you sign up: your email address and a hashed password. We never see your plain-text password.

When you use Proof: testimonials your customers submit, widget configurations, and basic usage logs (errors, API calls).

We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising.

Testimonial data

Testimonials submitted through your collection forms are stored in our database. You own this data. You can delete any testimonial at any time. If you delete your account, all your data is deleted within 30 days.

Cookies

We use a session cookie to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies. No third-party analytics on the core product (the landing page uses no analytics either).

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card number.

Contact

Questions? Email us: privacy@proof.app
