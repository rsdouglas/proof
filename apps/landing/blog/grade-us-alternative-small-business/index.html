Grade.us Alternative for Small Business: Simpler Testimonial Collection

Grade.us is a review management platform used primarily by marketing agencies to manage Google, Facebook, and other third-party reviews on behalf of multiple clients. It’s good at what it does — but for small business owners who want to collect customer testimonials directly and display them on their own website, it’s solving a different problem than the one you have.

What Grade.us Is Built For

Grade.us was designed for agencies managing reviews for many clients at once. Its core features include:

  • Sending automated review request campaigns via email and SMS
  • Monitoring third-party review sites (Google, Yelp, Facebook) across multiple locations
  • White-labeling for agencies to resell under their own brand
  • Reporting dashboards for client review performance

If you’re a digital marketing agency managing 20 local business clients, Grade.us makes sense. If you’re one of those local businesses, the tool is designed for your agency — not for you.

The Problem with Agency-First Tools for Small Businesses

When a platform is designed for agencies, the experience for the actual business owner is often:

  • Expensive — agency tools are priced for agencies billing clients, not solo operators
  • Complex — features you don’t need buried in a dashboard built for account managers
  • Focused on third-party review funneling — pushing customers to Google and Yelp, not collecting your own testimonials

There’s nothing wrong with wanting more Google reviews. But many small businesses also want testimonials they own and control — quotes they can put on their website, in their email signature, on social media — without depending on a third-party platform that could change its policies tomorrow.

What Small Businesses Actually Need

If you’re a small business owner who wants to collect customer testimonials, here’s the simple checklist:

  1. A shareable link you can send to customers asking for feedback
  2. A simple form customers can fill out in under 2 minutes
  3. A way to display testimonials on your website without touching code
  4. Free to start — you shouldn’t need to pay before you’ve proven the workflow

Grade.us charges per location and per campaign. For a single-location small business, you’re paying for infrastructure designed for a 50-location agency account.

How SocialProof Works as a Grade.us Alternative
