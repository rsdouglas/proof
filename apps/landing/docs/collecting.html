Collection link

Your collection link is the URL you share with customers to collect testimonials. It exists the moment you sign up — no setup required.

No widget needed to start collecting. Your collection link and testimonial pool are separate from widgets. Widgets are only for displaying testimonials on your site — you can collect first and embed later.

How it works

When you create a SocialProof account, you get a unique collection link:

https://collect.socialproof.dev/your-business-name

Send this link to a customer. They click it, fill out a short form (their name, role, and testimonial — takes under 2 minutes), and submit. The testimonial lands in your dashboard under Testimonials → Pending.

You review it, click Approve, and it's live — ready to display in any widget.

Where to share your link

📧 Email (most effective)

Example message Hi [Name] — working with you on [project] was a highlight. If you have 2 minutes, it would mean a lot if you'd share a quick testimonial: [your-collection-link]

No account needed, just a few sentences. Thank you!
Tip: The best time to ask is right after a win — project completion, a successful delivery, or a moment when the customer has just praised you. Strike while the feeling is fresh.

💬 Text or DM

Example message Hey! Quick favour — would you mind leaving a quick testimonial? It helps a lot: [your-collection-link] Takes 2 min, no account needed.

📝 Email signature

Add your collection link to your email signature so it's always present. Something like:

Signature line Happy with my work? Leave a quick testimonial → [your-collection-link]

🛒 Post-purchase or thank-you page

If you have an e-commerce store or booking platform, add your collection link to the order confirmation or thank-you page. This is the highest-volume, lowest-effort collection method — customers are at peak satisfaction right after a purchase.

📢 Social media

Post your collection link on LinkedIn, Instagram, or wherever your customers follow you. "I'm collecting feedback — if you've worked with me, I'd love a testimonial: [link]" gets real responses.

What customers see

The collection form is clean, mobile-friendly, and takes under 2 minutes to fill out. It asks for:

  • Their name
  • Their role or company (optional)
  • Their testimonial (free text)
  • Star rating (optional)

No account required. No email verification. They just fill it out and submit.

Moderating testimonials

All incoming testimonials go to Pending by default. You review and approve before anything goes public. You'll need to verify your email address before you can approve testimonials — check your inbox after signing up.

Once approved, testimonials are available in your testimonial pool and will appear in any widget you've configured.

Tips for getting more testimonials

  • Ask personally, not in bulk. A direct message to one person outperforms a mass email blast every time.
  • Make it easy. The fewer words in your ask, the better. "2 minutes, no account needed" removes friction.
  • Follow up once. A single gentle reminder 3–5 days later can double your response rate.
  • Ask right after a win. Timing is everything. Ask when the customer just praised you — don't wait a week.
