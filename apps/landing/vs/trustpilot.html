The Trustpilot problem nobody talks about

Your reviews live on their site

A Trustpilot badge links visitors away from your page. You've worked hard to get that review — it shouldn't drive traffic to someone else's domain.

You can't hide a bad review

Trustpilot is public. A disgruntled customer, a competitor, a bot — all their reviews appear alongside your best ones. No moderation control.

$299/mo to start

Trustpilot's cheapest paid plan is built for enterprise. If you're a small business or solopreneur, you're paying enterprise prices for a badge that links people away.

Verification takes days

Getting your business verified and your first reviews live takes significant back-and-forth. Vouch is running in under 10 minutes.
