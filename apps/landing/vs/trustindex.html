The key difference

✅ SocialProof is best for...

  • Businesses that want to collect fresh testimonials from real customers
  • Non-WordPress sites (Webflow, Squarespace, Shopify, custom)
  • Founders who want a simple tool, not a plugin ecosystem
  • Anyone who doesn't want to put in a credit card to try something

Trustindex is best for...

  • WordPress users who want to display existing Google/Facebook reviews
  • Businesses with strong existing review profiles they want to showcase
  • Teams already deep in the WordPress ecosystem

⚡ The biggest difference: collection vs. import

Trustindex pulls in reviews you already have. SocialProof helps you get more — send a shareable link to any customer, they type their testimonial in seconds. If your review count is low, SocialProof actively builds it up. Trustindex just displays what's already there.
