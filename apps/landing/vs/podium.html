What is Podium?

Podium is a customer communication platform built for local and multi-location businesses — think dental chains, auto dealerships, plumbers with 20 trucks. It bundles SMS marketing, review requests, web chat, team messaging, and payment collection into one dashboard.

That's genuinely powerful if you're running a 10-location franchise that needs one inbox for every location's text conversations. But for a single-location business or a freelancer who just wants customers' words on their website — it's a very expensive, very complex hammer for a small nail.

Frequently asked questions

Does SocialProof replace Podium for local service businesses?

It depends what you use Podium for. If you're using Podium mainly for review requests and displaying testimonials on your site, SocialProof is a direct and cheaper alternative. If you rely heavily on Podium's SMS inbox, team chat, and payment features, those aren't part of SocialProof — we're purely focused on collecting and displaying testimonials on your website.

Can SocialProof send review requests via SMS like Podium?

SocialProof uses shareable links — you paste or text the link to customers, and they fill in their testimonial via a web form. We don't send automated SMS blasts on your behalf, which keeps things simple and avoids TCPA compliance headaches. For most small businesses, a link sent via text is easier to set up than a Podium SMS campaign.

Podium has review widgets, but it's not their focus — their product is built around the inbox and review request flow. SocialProof's entire purpose is getting your customers' words onto your website in the best-looking, easiest-to-manage way possible.
