The honest verdict

BirdEye is genuinely good software — for the businesses it was designed for. Multi-location franchises, healthcare practices, and auto dealerships get real value from it. But if you're a solopreneur, a small e-commerce store, or a local service business with one location, BirdEye is overkill and overpriced by a factor of 30x.

SocialProof does one thing: helps you collect testimonials from happy customers and display them on your own website. No contracts. No sales calls. No reputation management complexity. Just social proof on your site, in 5 minutes.

What small businesses actually say about BirdEye

BirdEye users on G2 and Capterra commonly report:

  • Pricing shock — "We didn't realize the annual contract locked us in at $399/mo until we tried to cancel."
  • Feature overload — "We only needed testimonials on our site. We paid for a whole platform we didn't use."
  • Onboarding friction — "Required a sales call and a 2-week setup before we could do anything."

For a small business owner who just wants their three best testimonials on their homepage, that's a painful fit.

The SocialProof approach

We built SocialProof for a specific problem: small business owners have happy customers but no easy way to get those words onto their website. We stripped everything else out.

  1. Create a collection link (30 seconds)
  2. Send it to a happy customer via email or DM
  3. They fill a short form — no account, no friction
  4. You approve the testimonial in your dashboard
  5. It shows up on your site via a one-line embed
