The view-limit problem

Elfsight's free plan gives you 200 page views per month before your widget goes dark. Their Basic plan ($5/mo) allows 5,000 views. If your site gets real traffic, you'll quickly hit a wall and need to upgrade to Pro ($10/mo, 50,000 views) or Premium ($20/mo, 150,000 views).

SocialProof has no view limits. Your testimonial widget loads for every visitor, on every plan — including Free. When you upgrade to Pro at $9/mo, you get unlimited widgets, unlimited testimonials, analytics, and no caps. Ever.

SocialProof Pro is $9/mo flat. No view tiers, no widget caps, no overage charges. Know exactly what you'll pay.

The collection gap

The biggest difference most people miss: Elfsight doesn't help you collect testimonials. It's a display tool. You gather feedback somewhere else (email, Google Forms, DMs) and paste it in manually.

SocialProof gives you a unique collection link the moment you sign up. Share it with a customer, they fill out a short form, you approve it, and it appears in your widget automatically. No copy-pasting. No manual updates.

If you're trying to build a steady stream of fresh social proof, that difference matters.
