Different tools for different needs

Kudobuzz is built around social aggregation — pulling reviews from Facebook, Google, Twitter/X, and other platforms into one widget. If your business has an established presence on those platforms with hundreds of existing reviews, aggregation makes sense.

But if you want to proactively collect testimonials from customers — without asking them to log in to a review platform first — SocialProof is the better tool. You send a link. They write a few sentences. It appears on your site. No third-party platform required.

Who wins for small businesses?

✓ SocialProof is better if you…

  • Want to ask customers directly for testimonials
  • Don't have a large social review presence yet
  • Need to start for free with no credit card
  • Want full control over what appears on your site
  • Care about schema markup for search visibility

Kudobuzz might be better if you…

  • Already have many reviews on Facebook or Google
  • Want to aggregate and display reviews from multiple social platforms
  • Run a Shopify store with an existing review base
  • Need the social media feed/aggregation features
