Who should pick which tool?

✅ Pick SocialProof if you…

  • Sell services, not physical products
  • Run a coaching, consulting, or freelance business
  • Want testimonials on a Squarespace, Wix, or Webflow site
  • Don't need loyalty points, SMS, or Shopify integration
  • Want to start for free without a credit card

✅ Pick Yotpo if you…

  • Run a Shopify ecommerce store with real volume
  • Need product reviews, star ratings, UGC, and Q&A
  • Want loyalty and rewards integrated with your reviews
  • Need SMS marketing alongside reviews
  • Have budget for $15-$119+/month

If you're not on Shopify and you just want a polished testimonial widget, SocialProof is the faster, cheaper path.
