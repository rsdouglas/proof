The platform dependency risk

EmbedSocial's power — and its weakness — is that it relies on social platform APIs. If Instagram changes its API (it has, many times), your embedded feed breaks. If your Google business listing gets flagged, your reviews widget goes dark.

With SocialProof, you own the testimonials. They live in your account, not on a third-party platform. If you want to move, export them. There's no API integration that can break your site.

The no-social-media business

Not every small business is on Instagram or has a Google Business profile with reviews rolling in. A freelance consultant, a local coach, a B2B service provider — they often collect testimonials via email or conversation, not social platforms.

EmbedSocial has nothing to offer these businesses. SocialProof gives them a shareable link to send to any client, via any channel, and a widget to display the results — no social presence required.
