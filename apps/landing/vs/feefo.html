The Feefo model doesn't fit service businesses

Feefo's core product validates reviews by matching them to transactions in your order system. That works for a clothing retailer. It doesn't work if you're a therapist, a marketing consultant, a personal trainer, or a contractor. SocialProof's testimonial collection form lets any client leave a testimonial — no transaction matching required.
