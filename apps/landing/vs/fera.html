Who should pick which?

✅ SocialProof is best for...

  • Service businesses, coaches, consultants, freelancers
  • Any website that isn't a Shopify store
  • Businesses that want to proactively collect testimonials via a shareable link
  • Anyone who wants a simple, affordable, flat-rate tool

Fera.ai is best for...

  • Shopify merchants who want integrated product reviews
  • eCommerce stores that want "X people viewing this" and order-count widgets
  • Brands that need review request emails built into their Shopify flow

⚡ The key question: are you selling products or services?

Fera.ai is optimized for product-based eCommerce — Shopify order flows, review requests post-purchase, social proof counters. SocialProof is for any business that wants authentic customer testimonials and a clean way to display them. If you're a service business, SocialProof is the cleaner fit.
