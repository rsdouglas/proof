The text vs video tradeoff

Video testimonials are powerful when you can get them. The challenge: asking a client to film themselves is high friction. Most busy customers — even loyal, satisfied ones — won't do it. A simple text testimonial form gets you responses. An empty video testimonial library gets you nothing. SocialProof optimizes for volume and ease: collect lots of real testimonials you can actually use.

✓ SocialProof wins for…

  • Businesses that want high testimonial response rates
  • Service businesses where clients are too busy for video
  • Anyone starting free before committing to a paid tool
  • Businesses with Google/Yelp reviews to import
  • Budget-conscious solo founders and small teams

Vocal Video wins for…

  • Businesses with highly engaged, brand-enthusiast customers
  • SaaS or consumer products with passionate users
  • Companies that specifically need video for ads/social content
  • Brands where video production quality is a priority

The honest take

If you run a SaaS company with a rabid fanbase and you want video testimonials for your sales page and LinkedIn ads, Vocal Video might be worth $49/mo. It's a purpose-built tool for that use case.

If you're a plumber, a coach, a dentist, or a consultant who wants happy clients' words on your website — and you'd like those testimonials to actually show up — text testimonials collected via a simple form will outperform video every time on response rate.
