SocialProof vs EmbedReviews

Aggregate third-party reviews
or collect your own?

EmbedReviews pulls in reviews from Google, Facebook, and other platforms and displays them as a widget. SocialProof collects direct testimonials from customers without requiring review platforms. Different tools for different needs.

Try SocialProof free — no credit card

The fundamental difference

EmbedReviews is a review aggregator. It connects to your Google Business Profile, Facebook Page, Yelp, and other platforms and pulls those reviews into a widget for your site. If you already have reviews on those platforms and want to display them on your website, EmbedReviews solves that problem.

SocialProof is a testimonial collector. You send customers a link, they write their testimonial directly, you approve it, and it appears on your site. You're not dependent on any third-party platform. Customers don't need a Google account, Yelp account, or Facebook account to leave a testimonial.

If you have existing Google reviews you want to showcase: EmbedReviews is built for that. If you want to collect new testimonials from customers who don't use review platforms, or if you want testimonials that are more detailed and personal than a typical Google review: that's SocialProof's strength.

Who should use which tool?

✅ Pick SocialProof if you…

  • Want to collect new testimonials directly from customers
  • Serve customers who don't have Google/Yelp accounts
  • Run a B2B or professional services business
  • Want full control over every word that appears on your site
  • Don't want to depend on third-party platform API uptime
  • Are starting out and have few or no existing platform reviews

✅ Pick EmbedReviews if you…

  • Already have a strong Google Business Profile with many reviews
  • Want to display existing third-party reviews on your site
  • Run a local business where Google reviews are the gold standard
  • Need to pull from multiple platforms (Google + Facebook + Yelp) into one widget
