Two different approaches to social proof

Reviewflowz is a review monitoring and Slack notification tool. It watches your profiles on G2, Capterra, Google, and other platforms, then alerts you when new reviews come in and provides analytics dashboards. It's primarily a B2B SaaS tool for tracking what people say about you across the internet.

SocialProof is a testimonial collection and display platform. You send your customers a collection form, they submit their testimonials, you approve them, and they go live on your website automatically. Your visitors see real customer words right where it matters.

Who each tool is for

SocialProof is ideal for:

  • Small businesses and service providers
  • Anyone without a G2 or Capterra profile
  • Businesses wanting reviews on their own website
  • Early-stage companies building trust from scratch
  • Shopify sellers, freelancers, local service businesses
  • Anyone who wants free-to-start pricing

Reviewflowz is ideal for:

  • B2B SaaS companies with G2/Capterra presence
  • Companies managing reviews across many platforms
  • Customer success teams monitoring sentiment
  • Businesses needing Slack alerts for new reviews
  • Enterprise teams with dedicated review management budgets
$49/mo vs free.
If you're a small business wanting to display testimonials on your website, you don't need Reviewflowz's monitoring infrastructure. SocialProof is built for your use case and starts free.

Can you use both?

Yes — and some businesses do. If you're a B2B SaaS company that wants to monitor your G2 reviews and display first-party testimonials on your landing page, using Reviewflowz alongside SocialProof makes sense. They do genuinely different jobs.

But if you're a small business, consultant, or local service provider who just wants to turn happy customers into website social proof — SocialProof is the only tool you need, and it's free to start.

Frequently asked questions

Does SocialProof import reviews from Google or G2?

Currently SocialProof focuses on first-party testimonials collected directly from your customers. This gives you full control over the content and presentation. Google review import is on the roadmap.

What if I want both review monitoring and a website widget?

Start with SocialProof (free) for your website testimonials. If you grow to a point where you need to monitor your G2/Capterra presence, Reviewflowz can complement SocialProof for that specific use case.

Is SocialProof only for website embedding?

SocialProof's primary job is collection + display. You get a shareable collection form link (send it to customers), an approval dashboard, and an embeddable widget. It's end-to-end testimonial management, not review monitoring.

What's SocialProof's free plan?

Free forever for 1 active widget — unlimited testimonials collected and displayed. No credit card required. Pro is $9/mo for multiple widgets, custom branding removal, and priority support.
