When Stamped.io makes sense — and when it doesn't

Choose Stamped.io if…

  • You run a mid-size Shopify store with automated order review requests
  • You need photo/video reviews tied to product SKUs
  • You have a dev team to handle setup and integrations

Choose SocialProof if…

  • You run a service business: coaching, fitness, consulting, salons
  • You don't use Shopify (or want platform independence)
  • You want to collect testimonials today, not after a long setup
  • You need a free plan while you're getting started

Stamped.io is great product-review software for Shopify. But most small businesses aren't Shopify merchants — they're coaches, consultants, trainers, and service providers who just need a simple way to collect and display what their customers say. SocialProof is built for them.
