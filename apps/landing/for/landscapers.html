Landscaping and lawn care runs on reputation. Neighbors watch each other's yards — when a property suddenly looks immaculate, they want to know who did it. Landscaping is the most word-of-mouth industry there is

Testimonials bridge the gap: a homeowner who sees your sign + reads three glowing reviews on your website becomes a customer before they've even called.
