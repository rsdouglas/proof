Social Proof for Car Dealerships

Car buyers do 10+ hours of online research before they visit a dealership. By the time they walk through your door, they've already decided whether to trust you — based largely on what previous customers said. SocialProof helps dealerships collect genuine customer testimonials and display them where it matters: on your website, before the sale.

Why testimonials matter more than inventory

Every buyer knows you have cars. What they don't know is whether buying from YOU will be a good experience. Dealership reviews affect decisions more than most industries — a car is one of the biggest purchases a family makes. Authentic testimonials on your website preempt the anxiety and skepticism buyers carry into the showroom.

Testimonials that drive dealership sales

  • No-pressure experience: "I walked in dreading the usual sales tactics. Marcus listened, answered my questions, and never pushed. Best car buying experience I've had."
  • Fair pricing: "We agreed on a price quickly and it matched what I'd researched. No last-minute add-ons in the finance office."
  • Trade-in fairness: "They gave me $2,000 more for my trade than the other dealer. I drove home in my new car same day."
  • Service department: "Brought my car in for a recall. They had a loaner ready, finished same day, no charge. Phenomenal service."
  • Specific salesperson: "Ask for Jenna. She found me the exact trim I wanted in my color within 48 hours."

How SocialProof works for auto dealers

  1. Add the widget to your inventory pages, homepage, and service department page.
  2. After delivery, send buyers your SocialProof link via text — when they're still excited about their new car.
  3. Customers share naturally — no login, no app, just a simple text box.
  4. Review and approve before any testimonial appears on your site.
  5. Segment by department — sales testimonials on sales pages, service testimonials on the service page.
