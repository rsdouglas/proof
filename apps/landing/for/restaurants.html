Questions from restaurant owners

Is this different from Yelp or Google Reviews?

Yes. Yelp and Google reviews are public platforms you have no control over. SocialProof testimonials are collected directly from your guests and displayed on your own website. You approve every testimonial before it goes live.

How do I ask guests for testimonials without being pushy?

The best time is right after a great experience — a follow-up text or email 24 hours later. Something like: "It was great having you in. If you have 2 minutes, we'd love a few words: [link]." Our collection page is clean and takes under 2 minutes.

Will this work with my existing website?

Yes. SocialProof works with any website — Squarespace, Wix, WordPress, or a custom-built site. Paste one embed code and your testimonials appear automatically.

What does it cost?

Free for up to 25 testimonials and one widget — enough to get started and see results. Pro is $9/month for unlimited testimonials, unlimited widgets, and no SocialProof branding.

Can I collect testimonials from past guests I already have emails for?

Yes. SocialProof has a bulk request feature — paste up to 100 guest emails and send them all a testimonial request at once. Perfect if you have a reservation history or email list.

Do my guests need to create an account?

No. Guests click a link, type their testimonial, and hit submit. No sign-up, no password, no friction. That's why the completion rate is high.
