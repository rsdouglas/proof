Social Proof for Event Planners

Event planning is one of the highest-trust purchases a client makes. They're handing you a once-in-a-lifetime moment — a wedding, a milestone birthday, a corporate conference — and trusting you to execute it flawlessly. Before they sign anything, they read everything they can find about you. SocialProof helps you put your best client voices front and center, so that discovery process ends with them reaching out to you.

Why event planners live and die by testimonials

Photography shows what your events look like. Testimonials reveal what it actually felt like to work with you — how you handled the vendor who canceled at the last minute, how you soothed frayed nerves the morning of, how you made every guest feel the event was designed just for them. That's what converts a prospect into a client.

SocialProof gives you an embeddable testimonial widget that sits beautifully on your website, turning every completed event into a permanent, working advertisement.

What clients say when you've made their event perfect

  • Stress elimination: "I didn't worry about a single thing the entire day. She handled everything — I just got to enjoy it."
  • Crisis management: "The florist canceled 48 hours out. She had a replacement by the next morning. I never even knew until after."
  • Detail obsession: "Every tiny detail I mentioned in passing ended up in the final event. She actually listened."
  • Vendor management: "Every vendor showed up, everything ran on schedule. That's not luck — that's her."
  • Personal touch: "Felt like planning with a friend who happened to know every vendor in the city."

How SocialProof works for your planning business

  1. Embed the widget on your website — portfolio page, about page, or anywhere — in under 10 minutes.
  2. Send the collection link a few days after the event, when the high is still real and the gratitude is fresh.

The timing that gets heartfelt responses

Send your testimonial request 3–5 days after the event — not immediately, when clients are exhausted, and not weeks later, when the glow has faded. That sweet spot produces the kind of vivid, emotional testimonials that convert new prospects. Pair it with a personal note thanking them, and your response rate climbs dramatically.
