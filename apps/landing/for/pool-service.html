Why pool service companies need social proof

Homeowners searching for pool service are evaluating reliability above everything else. Will they show up? Will they actually fix problems? Will they try to upsell me on things I don't need? Testimonials from real clients answer these questions better than any ad or flyer ever could.

Testimonials that win pool service contracts

  • Reliability: "They've shown up every week for 3 years without missing a single visit. My pool is always perfect."

  • Equipment knowledge: "They diagnosed a failing heater before it failed completely. Saved the equipment and my summer."
