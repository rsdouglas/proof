Common Questions

Can patients submit anonymously?

Yes. Patients can submit using their first name only, a pseudonym, or "Anonymous Patient." You approve every testimonial before it goes live, so you control what's displayed.

Is this HIPAA compliant?

Vouch collects voluntary testimonials — not medical records or health data. Patients choose what to share. We recommend not asking patients to include specific diagnoses in public testimonials.
