Social Proof for Optometrists

Patients choose an eye doctor they trust with one of their most precious senses. SocialProof helps optometry practices collect genuine patient testimonials and display them prominently — so new patients feel confident before they ever call to book.

What makes optometry patients choose one practice over another

Most people in a new area search "optometrist near me" and read a few reviews before deciding. They're looking for signals: Does the doctor take time to explain? Do they recommend the right lenses, not just the most expensive ones? Is the staff friendly? Testimonials answer these questions in a way that credentials and photos never can.

Testimonials that fill your exam schedule

  • Trust in diagnosis: "She explained exactly why my prescription changed and what it means for my eye health."
  • Quality of care: "He detected early signs of glaucoma that two other doctors missed. Genuinely grateful."
  • Frame selection help: "They helped me find frames I actually love — first time in years I've liked how I look in glasses."
  • Contact lens fitting: "I'd struggled with contacts for years. She fit me in a different brand and it was life-changing."
  • Kid-friendly: "My 7-year-old was terrified. The whole team made it fun. He actually wants to come back."
