The unique trust challenge in speech therapy

💬

High emotional stakes

Parents aren't just choosing a provider — they're placing their child's developmental progress in your hands. Generic credentials don't reassure. Specific success stories do.

⏳

Long treatment timelines need early buy-in

Speech therapy takes months or years. Families who see evidence of real progress from other families commit more fully — and follow through on the homework.

🌐

School and insurance lists aren't enough

Being on a provider list doesn't differentiate you. Testimonials from parents who describe what made you different are what convert a referred name into a booked evaluation.

How SocialProof works for speech therapy practices

When a child says their first clear sentence, masters a sound, or hits a major milestone — that's when parents are most moved. Send your SocialProof link in that moment. The response rate is remarkable.
