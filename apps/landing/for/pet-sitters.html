Social Proof for Pet Sitters & Dog Walkers

Pet owners are trusting you with a family member and sometimes a house key. That decision doesn't happen without strong social proof. SocialProof helps you collect genuine client testimonials and display them on your website — so new clients arrive already confident they've found the right person.

Why trust is everything in pet care

Pet sitting and dog walking is an intensely personal service. Clients aren't just hiring someone for a task — they're trusting someone with their pet's wellbeing and sometimes access to their home. The barrier to a first booking is high, and it drops dramatically when you have visible proof from other pet owners who love you.

A testimonial widget on your website — showing real words from real clients and their dogs — answers the anxiety before it even forms.

What pet owner testimonials say that converts

  • Peace of mind: "She sent photo updates every few hours. I could actually relax on vacation for the first time."
  • Genuine care: "You could tell she actually loves dogs. Max was exhausted and happy every single walk."
  • Reliability: "Never missed a walk in 18 months. Always on time, always communicates."
  • Special needs: "Our senior dog needs medication twice a day — she handles it like a pro."

How it works

  1. After a pet sitting stay or a month of walks, send a SocialProof link to your client
  2. They submit a quick testimonial — and can include a photo of their pet
  3. You approve and it goes live on your website
  4. New clients find you and see a page full of happy pet owners before they send the first message
