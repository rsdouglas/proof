Social Proof for Cleaning Services

Someone is about to let you into their home. The only thing standing between "browsing" and "booking" is trust. SocialProof helps cleaning services, house cleaners, and maid services collect and display the testimonials that turn website visitors into recurring clients.

Trust is the only barrier to booking

Cleaning services have a unique challenge: clients need to trust you with their home, their valuables, and their family's daily environment. A Google listing with no reviews or an empty website testimonials section sends every prospect to your competitor — even if your work is excellent.

SocialProof makes it dead simple to collect written testimonials from your happy recurring clients and show them prominently on your booking page, homepage, and service pages.

The testimonials that convert cleaning prospects

  • Reliability: "They've never missed an appointment in 2 years. Not once."
  • Trustworthiness: "We gave them a key and never thought twice about it."
  • Thoroughness: "Got in corners my previous cleaner hadn't touched in months."
  • Communication: "They texted when they arrived and when they finished. Love that."
  • Results: "House smells amazing. I come home to a completely different space."

How it works for your cleaning business

  1. Embed the widget on your booking page and homepage — takes about 10 minutes, no coding needed.
  2. After every clean (or every few cleans for recurring clients), send the simple collection link via text or email.
  3. Approve and publish testimonials with one click — or enable auto-publish for trusted regulars.
  4. New visitors see real clients vouching for your reliability before they've even spoken to you.

Works for every cleaning business model

  • Residential house cleaning (recurring & one-time)
  • Move-in / move-out deep cleans
  • Airbnb & short-term rental turnovers
  • Post-construction cleanup
  • Commercial office cleaning
  • Specialty cleaning (carpets, windows, upholstery)
