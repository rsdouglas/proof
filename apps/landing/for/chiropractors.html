Great prompts to get specific testimonials

💬 Ask patients these questions when requesting a testimonial

  • "What were you dealing with before you came in, and what's different now?"
  • "Were you skeptical about chiropractic care before trying it?"
  • "How many sessions did it take before you felt a difference?"
  • "What would you tell a friend who's thinking about chiropractic but isn't sure?"
  • "Is there anything about the experience that surprised you?"

Specific answers convert skeptical visitors. "My back pain is gone" is vague. "I had a disc bulge at L4-L5 and after 8 weeks I'm back to running 5 miles" is specific and credible.
