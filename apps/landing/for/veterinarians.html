What great vet testimonials look like

✅ The stories that convert new clients

  • Specific pet name and situation: "Our cat Luna had an emergency at 6pm…"
  • Mentions compassion, not just competence: "The whole team made us feel calm"
  • Describes the outcome: "She fully recovered in 10 days"
  • Addresses cost or communication: "They explained every option before doing anything"
  • Compares to previous experiences: "After switching from our old vet, the difference is night and day"

When you ask for a testimonial, consider prompting: "What was your pet's situation when you came in, and what was your experience like?" Specific stories outperform generic praise every time.
