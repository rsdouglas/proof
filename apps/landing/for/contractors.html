The trust gap is costing you jobs

When a homeowner searches for a contractor — plumber, roofer, electrician, landscaper — they're not just comparing prices. They're trying to figure out who they can trust inside their home.

Most contractors rely on word-of-mouth referrals and Google reviews. But reviews live on Google's platform, not yours. A competitor with more reviews on their own website — in their own words, with real client names — wins the first impression every time.

The good news: collecting those testimonials is easier than you think. You just need to ask at the right moment and make it simple enough that clients actually follow through.
