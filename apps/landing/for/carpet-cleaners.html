The carpet cleaning trust problem

Carpet cleaning is a trust business. Customers are letting strangers into their home with equipment. They worry about hidden fees, upsells, wet carpets that take forever to dry, or a crew that damages their furniture. Every hesitation is a lost booking — unless social proof answers those fears before the phone rings.
