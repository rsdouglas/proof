Social Proof for Wedding Planners

Couples searching for a wedding planner are making one of the biggest emotional purchases of their lives. Before they schedule a discovery call, they spend hours reading everything they can find about you. Your Instagram is beautiful — but your testimonials are what make them pick up the phone. SocialProof helps you collect and display those testimonials automatically, so your website closes couples before you ever speak.

The testimonial is your most powerful sales tool

Portfolio photos show what you produce. Testimonials reveal who you are to work with — how you kept calm when the venue double-booked, how you made the bride's mom feel included, how you magically turned a rainy outdoor ceremony into something memorable. That's what separates you from every other planner with a beautiful website.

SocialProof gives you a simple widget that displays those stories right on your site — with zero technical setup and automatic updates as new testimonials come in.
