Add testimonials to Shopify in 4 steps

1

Create your free SocialProof account

Sign up at socialproof.dev. Your collection link is ready immediately — no configuration required.

2

Add your link to post-purchase emails

In Shopify, go to Settings → Notifications → Order confirmation. Add a line like "Love your order? Share your experience:" with your SocialProof link.

3

Approve incoming testimonials

As submissions arrive, review and approve the best ones from your SocialProof dashboard. They appear on your widget instantly.

4

Embed on your Shopify store

In your theme editor, add a Custom HTML section and paste your SocialProof script tag. Place it on your homepage, About page, or dedicated testimonials section.
